## Services Metier COULD add or adapt

### Expansion Opportunity A: Lender's Technical Advisory (LTA)
**What it is:** Independent technical DD for project finance lenders — reviews CAPEX realism, design adequacy, contractor capability, schedule achievability. Produces monthly drawdown recommendations.

**Current Metier gap:** This service exists in dc_services_all.md ("Potential for Metier: Yes") but is not in the 8-service public catalog. Metier has the KS2/QA methodology and owner-side advisory DNA; it just needs to reframe this for the lender-side context.

**Capability required:** Structured drawdown recommendation process; familiarity with project finance covenant structures; ability to produce monthly construction monitoring reports. This is ~80% existing Metier capability reframed.

**Credibility path:** Partner with a project finance lawyer (Wikborg Rein, BAHR) for the debt covenant advisory component; Metier provides the technical construction monitoring.

**Competitive whitespace:** Mitchell McDermott's Funder Advisory division is the benchmark (21+ sub-services). In Norway, this role is not yet systematically filled — a first-mover has a durable advantage.

**Value:** HIGH — recurring monthly engagement for the life of construction; direct PE/lender relationships open follow-on advisory with the borrower (operator).

---

### Expansion Opportunity B: DC-Specific Competence Program (Metier Academy DC Track)
**What it is:** A structured DC project management curriculum — MEP package management, commissioning planning, long-lead equipment procurement, Norwegian DC regulatory context — delivered via Metier Academy.

**Current Metier gap:** Metier Academy offers PRINCE2/PMI/agile but has no DC-specific content. This is a content gap, not a delivery capability gap.

**Capability required:** Develop 2–3 DC-specific modules; recruit 1–2 subject matter experts with DC PM track records to design and deliver the content.

**Competitive whitespace:** No Norwegian provider offers DC-specific PM training. International providers (BICSI, Uptime Institute AOS) offer DC technical training but not project management. This is an unclaimed niche.

**Value:** MEDIUM-HIGH — positions Metier as thought leader; feeds referrals from trained operators; creates recurring revenue from operators building internal PM teams.

---

### Expansion Opportunity C: Cleared PM Cadre for High-Security DC Segment
**What it is:** A team of Metier project managers who hold personal sikkerhetsklarering, allowing Metier to provide PM services for Pattern B (government/defense) DC projects.

**Current Metier gap:** Metier cannot currently operate inside cleared DC construction environments.

**Capability required:** Apply for relevant clearance levels for 3–5 identified PMs; maintain clearance management and compliance. Timeline: 6+ months per person. Cost: manageable.

**Competitive whitespace:** No Norwegian PM consultancy has systematically built this capability. The cleared DC construction market is growing (NSM requirements, NATO alignment, government cloud) and the advisory supply is structurally constrained.

**Value:** HIGH strategic — creates a durable moat in the Pattern B segment that no competitor can replicate quickly.

---

## Top 3 Expansion Opportunities (Ranked)

1. **Lender's Technical Advisory** — Highest immediate revenue potential; ~80% existing capability; opens PE/lender relationships; first-mover in Norway.
2. **Cleared PM Cadre** — Durable structural moat; Pattern B segment growing; limited competition; requires commitment but not major investment.
3. **Metier Academy DC Track** — Positions as thought leader; feeds referrals; unclaimed niche; builds brand before the market scales.
