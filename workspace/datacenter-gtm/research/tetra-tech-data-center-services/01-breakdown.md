# Breakdown: Tetra Tech Data Center Research

**Date:** 2026-04-18

## Context
Research Tetra Tech's data center capabilities globally, in Europe/Nordics, and Norway specifically for an internal briefing. Tetra Tech previously owned Metier and is now a potential alliance partner. Key risk: limited public information on Nordic/Norway-specific DC activities.

## Subtasks

1. **Map Tetra Tech's global DC service portfolio** — Review their website, capability statements, and published materials to document all data center services offered (design, engineering, construction management, sustainability, etc.)
2. **Research Tetra Tech's DC organizational structure** — Identify which division/business unit owns DC work, key practice leaders, and how the DC practice is structured globally
3. **Find Tetra Tech DC project references globally** — Identify publicly referenced data center projects, clients, and case studies — scale, type, geography
4. **Research Tetra Tech's European and Nordic DC presence** — Search for offices, staff, projects, or clients in the Nordics (Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland); document what is found and flag gaps
5. **Research Tetra Tech–Metier historical relationship** — Establish when Tetra Tech owned Metier, how the divestiture happened, and whether any formal ties remain
6. **Research Tetra Tech's DC market positioning** — How do they go to market for DC work? What partnerships, frameworks, or standards do they reference? Who are their typical clients?
7. **Synthesise: service overlap and alliance relevance** — Map Tetra Tech's DC services against Metier's service catalog; identify overlaps, gaps, and potential alliance angles (depends on 1–6)
