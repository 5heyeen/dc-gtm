# Breakdown: Norconsult Data Center Activities Globally

**Date:** 2026-04-17

## Context
Deep research into Norconsult's data center practice worldwide — covering services, reference projects, technical capabilities, org structure, and competitive positioning vs. other engineering consultancies. Key risks: DC work may have low public visibility, and the global scope is wide. All findings feed into Metier's Datacenter GTM strategy.

## Subtasks

1. **Identify Norconsult's DC business unit / practice area** — Find whether Norconsult has a named DC practice, which division owns it, and which geographies it operates in. Done when we have a clear org/practice map.

2. **Map DC service offerings** — Research the full range of services Norconsult markets to DC clients (site selection, design, MEP engineering, sustainability, construction management, commissioning, etc.). Done when we have a categorized service list with sources.

3. **Audit global DC reference projects** — Identify named projects, clients, locations, and scale. Done when we have ≥5 reference cases with source URLs.

4. **Assess technical capabilities and certifications** — Find any DC-specific credentials, standards they design to (Uptime Institute, EN 50600, LEED, BREEAM), specialist tools, or partnerships. Done when credentials/standards are documented.

5. **Research organizational footprint for DC work** — Identify which countries/offices lead DC delivery, headcount signals, and any recent hiring in DC roles. Done when we have a geographic delivery map.

6. **Analyse competitive positioning** — Compare Norconsult's DC positioning vs. Ramboll, Sweco, WSP, Arup, and Multiconsult. What does Norconsult claim to do differently? Done when we have a competitive differentiation summary.

7. **Map relevance to Metier's DC GTM strategy** — Synthesise findings against Metier's service catalog to identify where Norconsult is a competitor, a potential partner, or a reference point. Done when we have a 1-page strategic relevance summary.
