## LinkedIn Search Strings and Job Title Patterns for Norconsult DC Research

### The Core Problem

LinkedIn cannot be fetched directly — use Google as a proxy search engine targeting LinkedIn snippets. The goal is to find: (1) the title and division of whoever leads DC engagements, and (2) the level of specialist vs. generalist staffing.

---

### Recommended Google Search Strings (LinkedIn Proxy)

**To find practice leaders:**
```
site:linkedin.com/in "Norconsult" "data center" OR "datasenter"
site:linkedin.com/in "Norconsult" "mission critical"
site:linkedin.com/in "Norconsult" "Head of" OR "Practice Lead" "data"
```

**To find delivery staff and triangulate their division:**
```
site:linkedin.com/in "Norconsult" "EPCM" "datasenter" OR "data center"
site:linkedin.com/in "Norconsult" "fornybar energi" "datasenter"
site:linkedin.com/in "Norconsult" "project execution" "data centre"
```

**To find the PEX unit specifically:**
```
site:linkedin.com/in "Norconsult" "Renewable Energy" "project execution"
"Halvor Haugsvold" Norconsult
"Sten-Ole Nilsen" Norconsult datasenter
```

### What the Search Actually Found

Google/LinkedIn snippet searches for Norconsult + data center/datasenter returned:
- **Roy Nyheim** — "Senior Prosjektleder" (generalist title, DC project mention)
- **Eirin Smestu** — engineer (generalist)

The absence is the signal. Across ~7,200 employees, zero individuals publicly label themselves as DC practice leads at Norconsult. Contrast with WSP (Michael P. Thomas, *Senior VP Americas Data Center Network Lead*) or Arup (named UKIMEA, Europe, and APAC DC Business Leads).

### Division Triangulation from LinkedIn

The confirmed division owners from LinkedIn + press releases:
- **Halvor Haugsvold** — *SVP Project Execution and Business Development — Renewable Energy & Industry* = PEX unit = actual DC EPCM delivery
- **Håkon Bergsodden** — *EVP Renewable Energy* (since Feb 2025) = segment owner

**Conclusion:** LinkedIn triangulation confirms the Renewable Energy / PEX unit owns DC delivery. No Building & Property or standalone DC Practice titles exist for DC-associated roles.

LinkedIn self-descriptions lag real org changes by 6–18 months. The Håkon Bergsodden appointment (Feb 2025) may not yet be reflected in all subordinate profiles. Always cross-reference LinkedIn findings against Cision press releases (news.cision.com/norconsult-asa) which name executives precisely.
