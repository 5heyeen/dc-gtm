# Prompt 03: Norconsult DC Projects Beyond Bulk — Telemark, Hyperscalers, Other

> **Prompt:** Find Norconsult DC reference projects beyond the Bulk/Støleheia engagement. Research Google/Statkraft Telemark, Green Mountain, other hyperscalers, public sector, HPC, and Nordic-outside-Norway projects.

## Confirmed Projects (Beyond Bulk N01)

### Datasentral Telemark / Project Telemark — Gromstul, Skien
**(Confirmed — see prompt 01 for full details)**
Norconsult prepared planning programme (September 2017) and full regulatory plan + EIA for ~3,000 ha Gromstul site on behalf of Statkraft AS. Approved May 2018. Now Google's €600M / 240MW campus.

### Datasentral Sundland / Project Vestfold — Sandefjord
**(Confirmed — see prompt 01 for full details)**
Norconsult prepared regulatory plan + EIA + traffic analysis on behalf of Statkraft AS for ~128,000 m² / 120 MW potential. On hold at NVE.

### Statkraft Framework Agreement — Norway-wide
**Confidence: CONFIRMED (indirect DC relevance)**
Norconsult holds a renewed framework agreement with Statkraft for technical consulting in renewable power production. This framework underpins both DC site development engagements (Telemark and Vestfold).
- Source: https://norconsult.com/news/norconsult-renewed-framework-agreement-with-statkraft/

### Aure Næringsforum — Datasenter Feasibility, Hyttneset (Aure municipality)
**Confidence: CONFIRMED (early-stage feasibility)**
Norconsult engaged by Aure Næringsforum (ANF) to conduct a feasibility study on circular value chains at Hyttneset. Study explicitly included assessment of co-locating a data center alongside aquaculture, greenhouses, biogas, and algae production. Advisory/feasibility role only — no construction.
- Source: https://norconsult.no/aktuelt/landbasert-oppdrettsanlegg-datasenter-og-avokadoproduksjon-i-aure/

## DC-Adjacent Confirmed Projects (HV Power / Substation)

### INEOS Inovyn Rafnes — Power Supply Renewal, Grenland (Bamble)
**Confidence: CONFIRMED (DC-adjacent)**
Norconsult delivered full EPCM services for renewal of HV power supply to INEOS Inovyn's industrial campus at Rafnes — directly analogous to the Bulk Infrastructure engagement. Scope: project management, engineering, procurement, contract handling, construction management, systematic completion.
- Source: https://www.bygg.no/norconsult-far-kraftkontrakt-i-grenland/1540716!/

### Skillemoen Substation — Northern Norway (Statnett)
**Confidence: CONFIRMED (DC-adjacent)**
Norconsult served as designer for Jacobsen Elektro's EPC contract on the Skillemoen 420kV substation for Statnett — the first 420kV station in Finnmark. Design work February–December 2018. Confirms 420kV substation design capability at transmission level.
- Source: https://norconsult.com/projects/skillemoen-substation/

## Key Strategic Findings

1. **Norconsult is Statkraft's primary planning/regulatory consultant for DC site development in Norway** — both Telemark (Google's 240MW campus) and Vestfold. A significant repeating relationship.
2. **Norconsult's DC role is concentrated in planning/regulatory and HV power infrastructure** — not in project management of data halls, MEP fit-out, or commissioning.
3. **No evidence of Norconsult involvement with major Norwegian DC operators** beyond Bulk and Statkraft — no Green Mountain, no Lefdal, no STACK, no Skygard.
4. **No confirmed Nordic-outside-Norway DC projects** despite offices in all Nordic countries.
5. **The INEOS Inovyn and Skillemoen references confirm Norconsult's HV industrial EPCM capability** is the transferable skill driving their DC engagements.
