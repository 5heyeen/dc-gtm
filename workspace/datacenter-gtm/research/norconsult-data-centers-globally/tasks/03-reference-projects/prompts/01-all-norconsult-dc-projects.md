## Confirmed Projects

### PROJECT 1: N01 Datasentercampus — Støleheia, Vennesla (Agder)

**Client:** Bulk Infrastructure Group
**Location:** Støleheia, Vennesla municipality, Agder (near Kristiansand)
**Scale:** ~800,000 m² planned; site area >3,000 ha (~30 km²); investment >NOK 3B; site designed for eventual ~1 GW capacity
**Norconsult Scope (confirmed):**
- Full EPCM contract: project management and control, engineering management across all phases, procurement management, construction management, supervision of suppliers, HSE/SHA and systematic commissioning
- Specific engineering: design of two transformer stations (420kV/145kV), new 420kV transmission lines, 145/24kV transformers, associated switchgear, cable connections
**Phase:** Construction / EPCM execution
**Period:** Late 2023 – end 2026

### PROJECT 2: Datasentral Telemark (Google Gromstul) — Skien, Telemark

**Client:** Statkraft AS (site developer; Google / WS Computing AS is the end operator)
**Location:** Gromstul, ~6 km north of Skien, Telemark
**Scale:** ~600,000 m² buildable floor area; 4 km² plan area; Google investment €600M (NOK ~6.8B); 860 MW applied for
**Norconsult Scope (confirmed):**
- Prepared the planning programme (planprogram), June 2017, on behalf of Statkraft AS
- Prepared full regulatory plan (reguleringsplan) + impact assessment (konsekvensutredning) for Statkraft AS
- Plan approved by Skien City Council: May 31, 2018 (initial); revised plan approved September 26, 2023
**Phase:** Feasibility / Land use planning / Regulatory
**Period:** 2017–2018 (initial); 2023 revision

Note: Norconsult's project page lists this as "Datasentral Telemark." Same Gromstul site where Google is currently constructing (Skanska NOK 1.7B contract).

Sources:
- https://norconsult.no/prosjekter/datasentral-telemark/

### PROJECT 3: Project Vestfold / Datasentral Sundland — Sandefjord, Vestfold

**Client:** Statkraft AS
**Location:** Sundland, Sandefjord municipality, Vestfold (near E18 and Torp Airport)
**Scale:** ~128,000 m² building footprint; up to 120 MW connection potential
**Norconsult Scope (confirmed):**
- Prepared full regulatory plan (reguleringsplan) + impact assessment (KU) on behalf of Statkraft AS

**Phase:** Feasibility / Land use planning / Regulatory

**Status:** On hold at NVE level — concession process suspended pending Statnett grid capacity decision.

## Key Observations

1. **Two distinct service lines:** (a) Land-use/regulatory planning for site developers — Projects 2 and 3, both for Statkraft; (b) Full EPCM for construction — Project 1 for Bulk Infrastructure.
2. **Statkraft relationship:** Norconsult was the go-to consultant for at least two Statkraft DC site development efforts.
3. **No confirmed DC projects outside Norway:** No evidence found in Sweden, Denmark, Finland, or Iceland.
4. **No direct hyperscaler contracts:** Norconsult's hyperscaler exposure has been indirect (planning for Statkraft which sold land to Google).
5. **Project 1 is the only confirmed live execution contract.** Projects 2 and 3 were planning/regulatory assignments.
6. **Only 3 named DC projects publicly verifiable** despite the website claim of "several projects for sustainable data centers."
