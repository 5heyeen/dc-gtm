## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**The opportunity:** Norway's DC construction market is $3B+ annually (2025) and growing at 21% CAGR, driven by AI demand (OpenAI/Stargate, Bitdeer), hyperscaler expansion (Google), and PE-backed development (Bulk/BGO, Lefdal/3i, Stack/Apollo). Statnett has 3.4 GW of DC grid reservations — 53 projects representing >8% of national installed generation capacity. This is a multi-decade infrastructure buildout, not a cyclical peak.

**Our position:** Metier has active DC engagements (Bulk, Green Mountain, Google). Norconsult has engineering relationships with 8+ DC operators and a unique grid connection EPCM capability. Combined, we have relationships with the majority of active Norwegian DC operators — and a competitive advantage no other firm can replicate: integrated PM + engineering + grid connection in one organization, with the deepest Norwegian market knowledge in the industry.

**The gap we're filling:** No firm in Norway currently offers investor-grade DC execution advisory backed by a national-scale engineering firm. Local competitors (Rambøll, Sweco, Multiconsult) have Norwegian engineering but not DC-specific PM/advisory or investor-facing capability. We occupy the white space.

## 7. DECISION REQUIRED

The steering group should choose one of three strategic options:

### Option A: "Invest for Growth" (Recommended)
- **Action:** Formally establish joint DC practice. Dedicate BD resource. Pursue all 5 Tier 1 accounts. Build investor advisory capability. Target NOK 60M Year 3.
- **Investment:** NOK 3.5M/year
- **Risk:** Moderate — existing client revenue covers investment
- **Upside:** Market-leading position in the fastest-growing Norwegian infrastructure segment
- **Why now:** Lefdal (3i) and Stack (Apollo) ownership transitions create time-limited windows. OpenAI/Stargate is putting Norway on the global DC map. First-mover advantage matters.

### Option B: "Organic Growth"
- **Action:** Continue serving existing clients. Expand opportunistically. No dedicated BD. No formal practice.
- **Investment:** Minimal (NOK 500K-1M for training/content)
- **Risk:** Low financial risk, HIGH opportunity cost
- **Upside:** NOK 20-30M Year 3 (existing clients + occasional new)
- **Downside:** Competitors fill the gap. We remain a project-level vendor, not a strategic DC partner.

### Option C: "Partner First"
- **Action:** Establish partnerships with international DC PM firms (Mitchell McDermott, Linesight). Position as "Norwegian execution layer." Build references through partnership before going direct.
- **Investment:** NOK 1-2M/year (partnership development, co-marketing)
- **Risk:** Depends on partner willingness. Risk of permanent junior-partner positioning.
- **Upside:** Lower risk path to DC references. Learning curve via partnership.
- **Downside:** Slower revenue. Shared margin. Risk of never graduating to lead advisor.

**Recommendation: Option A.** The market timing, the Norconsult integration, and the existing client relationships create a window that won't stay open. The investment is modest relative to the opportunity and is already covered by existing revenue. Waiting gives competitors time to fill the gap.
