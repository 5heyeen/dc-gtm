## 2029-2030: FUTURE — Market Maturation

### Market Shape at Steady State:
- Norwegian DC market at $2-3B annual, steady growth
- Multiple GW of operational capacity
- Second generation of builds: expansions, upgrades, brownfield conversions
- Established contractor market with DC-specific capability
- Regulatory framework mature (EED fully transposed, Taxonomy compliance routine)

### Does Consulting Demand Increase or Decrease?

| Factor | Impact on Consulting Demand |
|--------|---------------------------|
| More projects | INCREASES total demand |
| Higher project complexity (AI/liquid cooling) | INCREASES per-project demand |
| Operators building internal capability | DECREASES per-operator demand |
| Mature contractor market | DECREASES contractor management needs |
| Regulatory compliance (ongoing) | Creates STEADY demand |
| Brownfield/upgrade cycle | Creates NEW demand stream |
| Program-level operations advisory | NEW demand category |

**Net assessment:** Total consulting TAM GROWS but individual operator spend may plateau as internal capability matures. The key is to build durable client relationships and institutional knowledge that keeps Metier + Norconsult essential even as the market matures.

### Best Long-Term Service Categories:
1. **Grid connection EPCM** — every new build needs it, Norconsult has competitive moat
2. **Program management** — multi-site operators always need governance
3. **Investor advisory** — PE fund lifecycle creates repeat demand across deals
4. **Regulatory navigation** — ongoing compliance creates steady demand
5. **Sustainability/ESG** — growing regulatory requirements, EU Taxonomy alignment
6. **Brownfield/upgrades** — lifecycle creates recurring work as facilities age or technology changes
