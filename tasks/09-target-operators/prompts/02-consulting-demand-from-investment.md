## 1. CONSULTING SPEND AS % OF CAPEX

### International Benchmarks

| Service Category | % of CAPEX (International) | % of CAPEX (Norway est.) | Rationale for Norway Premium |
|-----------------|---------------------------|-------------------------|----------------------------|
| PM / Owner's Representative | 1.5-3% | 2-4% | Norwegian market complexity, NS contracts, contractor coordination |
| Engineering Design | 3-6% | 4-7% | Local code compliance, design adaptation, Norconsult-type scope |
| Grid Connection Advisory/EPCM | 0.5-2% | 2-4% | Norway-specific: NVE concession, substation EPCM, Statnett coordination — unique to Norway |
| Regulatory / Permitting | 0.5-1% | 1-2% | Norwegian-specific: municipal permitting, environmental impact, labor regulations |
| Pre-FID Advisory | 0.3-0.5% | 0.5-1% | Higher in new markets — operators need Norwegian execution validation |
| Program Management | 0.5-1% | 1-2% | Multi-site operators need portfolio governance |
| Commissioning / Handover | 0.3-0.5% | 0.5-1% | Norwegian compliance verification at handover |
| Sustainability / ESG | 0.2-0.5% | 0.5-1% | Growing: EU Taxonomy, CSRD, spillvarme requirements |
| **TOTAL ADVISORY** | **7-14%** | **11-22%** | **Norway premium: local market complexity + regulatory navigation** |

**Variation by operator type:**
- **Hyperscalers:** Lower % (2-5% advisory total) — large internal teams, proven playbooks
- **PE-backed developers:** Higher % (10-20%) — less internal capability, investor governance requirements
- **First-time Norway entrants:** Highest % (15-22%) — maximum need for local market navigation
- **Domestic operators scaling:** Medium % (5-10%) — know Norway, need capacity and program capability

## 3. ADDRESSABLE MARKET FOR METIER + NORCONSULT

### From TAM to SAM to SOM

| | Definition | Estimate | Basis |
|---|-----------|----------|-------|
| **TAM** | All DC advisory services in Norway | NOK 2.0-4.0B/year | Based on CAPEX × advisory % |
| **SAM** | Services we can deliver + operators we can access | NOK 500M-1.2B/year | ~30% of TAM — limited by: services we offer (PM, engineering, grid, regulatory — not IT design, not MEP installation), operators we have access to (8 of top 11 via Norconsult), competitive intensity |
| **SOM** | Realistic win rate | NOK 25-90M/year | ~5-8% of SAM — competitive market, building practice from small base |

### SAM by Service Category

| Service | In our SAM? | Rationale |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Engineering Design | **YES** (Norconsult) | Core Norconsult capability. Already doing this for 8+ DC operators. |
| PM / Owner's Representative | **YES** (Metier) | Core Metier capability. Already doing for Bulk and Green Mountain. |
| Grid Connection Advisory/EPCM | **YES** (Norconsult) | Unique competitive advantage — very few firms can do NVE concessions + substation EPCM. |
| Pre-FID Advisory | **YES** (Metier + Norconsult) | Investor-grade execution readiness. New capability to build/package. |
| Program Management | **YES** (Metier) | Natural extension of PM to multi-site operators. |
| Regulatory/Permitting | **YES** (Norconsult) | Environmental impact, area planning, municipal processes. |
| Investor Advisory (LTA) | **BUILD** (Metier + Norconsult) | New service to develop. Gap in Norwegian market. |
| Commissioning | **PARTIAL** | Can do governance/oversight. Specialist commissioning agents needed for MEP testing. |
| Sustainability/ESG | **PARTIAL** (Norconsult) | BREEAM, energy calculations existing. EU Taxonomy/CSRD advisory to develop. |
| MEP Design/Installation | **NO** | Not in scope — this is contractor territory. |
| IT Infrastructure Design | **NO** | Not in scope — this is DC-specific technical (cooling architecture, white space design). |
