## HYPERSCALERS BUILDING IN NORWAY

### 1. Google (Gromstul/Skien)

**Capital Position:** Hyperscaler balance sheet (Alphabet). €600M ($646M) invested in Skien facility. Self-funded from corporate balance sheet — no external financing constraints.
- Source: [DLA Piper](https://www.dlapiper.com/en-us/insights/publications/2024/05/construction-of-googles-eur600-million-hyperscale-data-centre-in-norway), [Sultan Qureshi](https://sultanqureshi.co.uk/googles-e600m-investment-in-norway-a-game-changer-for-data-centers/)

**Demand Position:** 100% own workloads (cloud, AI, enterprise). Build-to-own, not speculative. Immediate need is 100 MW with additional 20 MW pending approval. Future expansion to 240 MW total.
- Source: [Datacenter Forum](https://www.datacenter-forum.com/google/google-started-building-240mw-data-center-in-norway)

**Site Position:** 200-hectare (2,000 decare) site in Gromstul area, Skien municipality, acquired in 2019. Nearly 2,000 decares — massive expansion optionality. Greenfield site. 240 MW allocated for Phase 1.
- **Powered land strategy:** Greenfield near existing grid infrastructure. Google secured land years before construction.
- Source: [DCD](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/google-breaks-ground-on-norway-data-center/), [IT Pro](https://www.itpro.com/infrastructure/data-centres/google-breaks-ground-on-its-first-norwegian-data-center-as-tech-giant-targets-99-carbon-free-operations)

**Power Position:** 240 MW Phase 1. Grid connection confirmed. Norconsult involved in grid connection work via Skagerak Nett/Statkraft for the Gromstul switching station (132 kV).
- Source: Norconsult presentation (provided context)

**Connectivity:** Skien is well-connected to Oslo area fiber networks.

**Organizational Readiness — Local & International:**
- **International org:** Google's global DC development HQ in the US. Centralized design standards deployed globally. Google uses its own global delivery playbook.
- **Local execution:** Google engaged Martinsen & Duvholt and Skanska as contractors. Norconsult provided Norwegian adaptation — VA, structural, HVAC, electrical, geotechnics, hydrology. This confirms Google uses international designs adapted to Norwegian standards.
- **Consultants:** Norconsult (engineering/local adaptation), Skanska (construction). No public evidence of Mitchell McDermott or Hill International involvement, but hyperscalers typically bring international PM firms.
- **Friction points:** International design needed adaptation to Norwegian conditions, standards, and requirements — this is exactly the "localization gap" that creates demand for local advisory.
- Source: Norconsult presentation

**Lifecycle Phase (March 2026):** Construction underway, targeting Q4 2025 / 2026 completion. Moving toward commissioning.

**Persona:** Segment 2 — Global Operators entering Norway

### 2. Microsoft / Azure

**Capital Position:** Hyperscaler balance sheet. Microsoft has committed $6.2 billion to datacenter infrastructure deals involving Norway (via partnership with Nscale/Aker at Narvik — see Nscale entry). Norway East is the established Azure region.
- Source: [Business Norway](https://businessnorway.com/articles/mature-norwegian-data-centre-industry-supports-ai-explosion)

**Demand Position:** Own workloads (Azure cloud + AI). Norway East region operational. Stavanger region discontinued — consolidating around Oslo-based Norway East.
- Source: [DCD](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/microsoft-launches-availability-zones-in-norway-east-azure-cloud-region/)

**Site Position:** Existing Norway East facilities in the Oslo region. Availability Zones launched. No publicly announced major new greenfield site in Norway beyond existing region.

**Power Position:** Existing grid connections for Norway East. For AI workloads, Microsoft is partnering with Nscale/Aker for the Narvik Stargate Norway facility (230 MW expandable to 520 MW).

**Organizational Readiness:** Well-established Norwegian presence through Azure Norway East. Microsoft typically uses global EPCM contractors and brings international PM firms.

**Lifecycle Phase:** Operational (Norway East), with expansion through partnership (Narvik/Stargate).

**Persona:** Segment 2 — Global Operators

### 8. OpenAI / Nscale / Aker — **MAJOR NEW ENTRANT**

**Capital Position:** 50/50 JV between Nscale and Aker. ~$1 billion committed to initial 20 MW phase (including ~$250M equity). OpenAI confirmed USD 2 billion commitment for the Stargate campus. Microsoft involvement with $6.2 billion deal for 230 MW (expandable to 520 MW) at Kvandal.
- Source: [Nscale Press Release](https://www.nscale.com/press-releases/stargate-norway-nscale-aker-openai), [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-stargate-norway/), [DCD](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/openai-partners-with-nscale-and-aker-for-stargate-norway-data-center-with-100k-gpus/)

**Demand Position:** AI training/inference. 100,000 NVIDIA GPUs targeted by end of 2026. OpenAI is the anchor customer.

**Site Position:** Two sites:
1. **Stargate Norway / Kvandal (Narvik):** 230 MW, expandable to 520 MW (230+290 MW). Access to abundant hydropower, cool climate, industrial infrastructure.
2. **Korgen:** 250 MW planned, on land formerly owned by Nordkraft. Still waiting for Statnett power allocation.
- Source: [High North News](https://en.highnorthnews.com/business/aker-nscale-planning-another-ai-data-center-in-northern-norway/118322)

**Power Position:** Narvik site — hydropower access confirmed. Korgen — awaiting Statnett grid allocation. Both in Northern Norway where power is more available.

**Powered land strategy:** Acquiring former industrial/energy company land with existing grid proximity (Nordkraft land in Korgen).

**Organizational Readiness:**
- **Aker:** Norwegian industrial conglomerate with deep infrastructure expertise, project management capability, and political connections. Aker has extensive Norwegian construction/industrial project experience.
- **Nscale:** AI infrastructure company. Also expanding Glomfjord data center with 30 MW building.
- Source: [DCD](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/nscale-to-expand-glomfjord-data-center-in-norway-with-30mw-building/)

**Lifecycle Phase:** Early construction (Narvik), pre-FID/development (Korgen).

**Persona:** Segment 9 — High-density AI/HPC compute specialists (with Segment 1 "Finansgutta" elements via Aker's investment structure)

## COLOCATION / WHOLESALE PROVIDERS

### 9. Bulk Infrastructure

**Capital Position:**
- €350M equity investment from BGO (formerly BentallGreenOak, a SunLife company), announced 2024. Plans for over €1 billion in investments by 2026.
- €410M senior secured loan closed November 2025 for N01 campus expansion.
- Source: [BGO](https://bgo.com/press-release/bulk-infrastructure-announces-350msup1/sup-equity-investment-by-bgo-and-plans-for-over-1bn-of-investments-by-2026-to-grow-sustainable-data-centre-l-1717514270601), [Bulk](https://bulkinfrastructure.com/newsroom/bulk-infrastructure-secures-410-million-for-n01-data-center-campus-expansion)

**Demand Position:** Wholesale/colocation. Tenants not publicly disclosed but serving hyperscale and enterprise customers. Building speculative + pre-committed capacity.

**Site Position:**
- **N01 Data Center Campus, Støleheia/Vennesla (Kristiansand):** 300 hectares of zoned land. Currently 400 MW by 2026, with ambitions for 2 GW medium-term. Proximity to one of Northern Europe's largest transformer stations.
- Additional expansion sites in Oslo area.
- **Powered land strategy:** Strategic greenfield near major existing transformer station infrastructure.
- Source: [Bulk](https://bulkinfrastructure.com/newsroom/bulk-data-centers-expands-norwegian-n01-data-center-campus), [DatacenterMap](https://www.datacentermap.com/norway/kristiansand/no1/)

**Power Position:** 400 MW by 2026. Collaborating with grid companies to add 300 MW in 2026. Norconsult involved in 420 kV grid connection license application and EPCM for two substations.

**Organizational Readiness:** Norwegian company with established DC delivery capability. Metier currently provides project controls, project execution framework, and board support. Norconsult provides engineering.

**Lifecycle Phase:** Active construction and expansion. Multi-site program.

**Persona:** Segment 3 — Domestic Operators scaling (with Segment 1 elements given PE backing from BGO)

**Organizational Readiness:** Experienced Norwegian operator. Multi-site. Metier provides project management. Norconsult provides engineering across sites.

## MARKET OVERVIEW DATA

**Total Norway DC market:**
- 35 existing data centers, 27 upcoming (2026 report)
- 30 existing, 20 upcoming (2025 report)
- 24 major operators/investors
- Existing capacity ~300 MW at full build
- Statnett has received 3,300 MW of DC grid reservation requests — 53 DCs registered, 3.4 GW reserved (>8% of Norway's installed generation capacity)
- Norway hyperscale DC market: $697M (2025) → $2,064M (2031), 19.83% CAGR
- Oslo and Grimstad lead upcoming development (>50% of planned power capacity)
- Sources: [GlobeNewswire 2026](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/03/11/3253730/28124/en/), [GlobeNewswire 2025](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/01/06/3213353/28124/en/), [Mordor Intelligence](https://www.mordorintelligence.com/industry-reports/norway-hyperscale-data-center-market), [TechPolicy Press](https://www.techpolicy.press/despite-plenty-of-renewable-energy-data-centers-split-norwegian-society/)

**Powered Land Acquisition Strategies Observed:**
1. **Greenfield near major transformer stations:** Bulk N01 (near Northern Europe's largest transformer station)
2. **Former industrial land with grid access:** Nscale/Aker Korgen (former Nordkraft land), Mo Industripark
3. **Mine conversions:** Lefdal Mine (olivine mine)
4. **Municipal/industrial park co-location:** Colonor/Kilemoen, Stack/Ringerike (Treklyngen industrial park)
5. **Utility-owned land:** Statkraft offering "powered sites"
6. **Former mining/bitcoin operations:** Bitdeer Tydal (converting mining facility to AI DC)
7. **Proactive municipal zoning:** Multiple municipalities actively marketing DC-ready sites
