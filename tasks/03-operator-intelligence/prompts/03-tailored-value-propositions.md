## 1. BULK INFRASTRUCTURE — EXISTING CLIENT (EXPAND)

**Hook:** "You're scaling from a 100 MW campus to a 2 GW program. The project-level controls we've been doing need to become program-level governance — before BGO's next board meeting asks why the second phase costs more per MW than the first."

**Value Proposition:**
1. **Program governance framework** — evolve from project-level controls to campus program management (stage gates, portfolio reporting, resource allocation across concurrent builds)
2. **Investor-grade reporting** — BGO requires institutional-quality cost/schedule reporting. Metier designs the framework, runs the controls.
3. **Grid connection PM overlay** — Norconsult is doing EPCM for the substations. Metier provides owner's-side PM to ensure grid infrastructure and DC construction programs are synchronized.
4. **Contractor strategy** — program-level contractor management in a tight Norwegian MEP market. Framework agreements, capacity reservation, performance management.

**Entry Point:** Expand current engagement from project controls to program governance. Propose a "Program Management Office (PMO) Design" engagement — 6-8 week scoping exercise to design the governance framework for the 1 GW campus program.
- Scope: PMO design, program reporting framework, contractor strategy, cost benchmarking
- Duration: 6-8 weeks for design, then ongoing PMO operation
- Team: 2-3 senior consultants
- Fee: NOK 1.5-2.5M for design; NOK 5-10M/year for ongoing PMO

**Relationship Map:** Metier already has direct client relationship. Decision-maker: likely Head of Development / CEO. BGO (investor) may also influence governance requirements.

**Competitive Situation:** Low competitive threat — Metier is incumbent. Risk is scope creep or Bulk building internal PMO capability.

**Opportunity Sizing (3 years):**
- Low: NOK 15M (maintain current scope)
- Base: NOK 30-40M (expand to program governance + grid PM overlay)
- High: NOK 50-60M (full PMO + investor reporting + contractor strategy)

## 2. GREEN MOUNTAIN — EXISTING CLIENT (EXPAND)

**Hook:** "You're running three simultaneous site programs with different tenants, different risk profiles, and different timelines. The learning from Hamar should be systematically captured and deployed to Enebakk and Rjukan — and whatever comes next."

**Value Proposition:**
1. **Multi-site delivery framework** — standardized project delivery model across sites with lessons-learned capture
2. **TikTok expansion PM** — if 150 MW option exercised, construction PM for Phase 2 at Hamar
3. **New site development advisory** — site selection and feasibility support for next campus
4. **Commissioning governance** — systematic handover framework for tenant onboarding

**Entry Point:** Propose a "Delivery Framework Standardization" project — codify the Hamar delivery approach into a repeatable model.
- Scope: Document current delivery model, identify improvements, create templates and stage gates
- Duration: 4-6 weeks
- Team: 2 senior consultants
- Fee: NOK 1-1.5M

**Relationship Map:** Metier has direct client relationship. Norconsult works across multiple sites.

**Competitive Situation:** Low threat — incumbent. Green Mountain is a Norwegian operator who values Norwegian advisors.

**Opportunity Sizing (3 years):**
- Low: NOK 10M (maintain current scope)
- Base: NOK 20-30M (framework + expansion PM)
- High: NOK 40M (full multi-site program management)

## 3. LEFDAL MINE DATA CENTER (3i Infrastructure) — HIGH PRIORITY NEW

**Hook:** "3i is about to close a €300M acquisition of a facility with 200 MW of potential but only 37 MW operational. You need an independent Norwegian execution readiness assessment before you approve the expansion CapEx plan — and the team that did the original grid concession is already on our bench."

**Value Proposition:**
1. **Pre-FID execution readiness assessment** — investor-grade independent review of the expansion plan, cost estimates, schedule, and risks. Exactly the deliverable a PE fund needs before approving expansion CapEx.
2. **Grid connection advisory** — Norconsult did the original 132 kV concession and transformer station. Extension for expanded capacity.
3. **Mine-specific construction governance** — unique underground DC requires specialized PM (phased excavation, rock reinforcement, ventilation systems)
4. **Norwegian regulatory navigation** — NVE, municipal permitting, environmental for expansion

**Entry Point:** Approach 3i's investment team (not just the operating company) with a "Norwegian DC Execution Readiness Assessment" proposal, timed to the ownership transition closing (summer 2026).
- Scope: Independent review of expansion plan, cost validation, schedule risk, Norwegian market assessment, grid infrastructure review
- Duration: 4-6 weeks
- Team: 3-4 (Metier PM + Norconsult engineers)
- Fee: NOK 1.5-2.5M (investor-grade deliverable commands premium pricing)

**Relationship Map:** Norconsult has deep existing relationship with Lefdal (concessions, structures, geotechnics, ventilation work since 2018). Entry to 3i investment team may require introductions through financial advisory networks or through the operating company.

**Competitive Situation:** Strong position — Norconsult is the incumbent engineering advisor. 3i may bring their own technical advisor, but Norconsult's site-specific knowledge is irreplaceable. Risk: 3i uses a global firm (Turner & Townsend, Faithful+Gould) for the investment assessment, with Norconsult as a technical sub-input.

**Opportunity Sizing (3 years):**
- Low: NOK 5M (execution assessment only)
- Base: NOK 15-25M (assessment + ongoing expansion PM + grid advisory)
- High: NOK 30-40M (full program management for multi-level expansion + investor monitoring)

## 4. GOOGLE (Gromstul) — EXPAND VIA NORCONSULT

**Hook:** "Phase 2 will need the same Norwegian adaptation work as Phase 1 — but this time, you can have the engineering and PM coordination integrated from day one instead of discovering interface gaps mid-build."

**Value Proposition:**
1. **Integrated PM + engineering for Phase 2** — Norconsult's engineering + Metier's PM as a single team, eliminating the coordination overhead of separate firms
2. **Norwegian contractor coordination** — managing the growing pool of Norwegian contractors who now have Google DC experience from Phase 1
3. **Grid expansion advisory** — additional capacity beyond 240 MW requires new grid infrastructure

**Entry Point:** Through Norconsult's existing relationship. Propose integrated PM for Phase 2 expansion.
- Scope: Owner's representative / PM overlay for Norwegian execution
- Duration: 18-24 months (construction phase)
- Team: 3-5 (embedded PM team)
- Fee: NOK 8-15M

**Relationship Map:** Norconsult has direct relationship. Google's decision on PM partner likely made at regional/global level. Need to reach VP Construction or equivalent — through Norconsult contacts or through Google's Norwegian team.

**Competitive Situation:** Google may use their global PM firm. Metier's angle: "we already have the engineering team embedded — adding PM creates a single integrated Norwegian partner." Risk: Google prefers its existing global PM firm + Norconsult continues as engineering sub.

**Opportunity Sizing (3 years):**
- Low: NOK 3M (Norconsult engineering only, no Metier involvement)
- Base: NOK 15-20M (integrated PM + engineering for Phase 2)
- High: NOK 30M+ (campus program management if Google commits to multi-phase expansion)

## 5. OPENAI / NSCALE / AKER (Stargate Norway) — STRATEGIC TARGET

**Hook:** "Norway's largest AI infrastructure project is being built in Northern Norway, where the DC construction market doesn't exist yet. You need someone who knows how to build complex industrial facilities in remote Norwegian locations — and who can bridge the gap between Nvidia's reference designs and Norwegian building codes."

**Value Proposition:**
1. **Northern Norway execution intelligence** — contractor market, logistics, labor regulations, environmental considerations specific to the north
2. **Norwegian code compliance** — adapting Nvidia/Nscale reference designs to Norwegian electrical codes, building regulations, fire safety
3. **Grid connection support** — NVE concession and grid infrastructure for Korgen (awaiting Statnett allocation)
4. **Cost benchmarking** — Norwegian market cost estimates for an AI-specific facility that has no Norwegian precedent

**Entry Point:** Through Aker's Norwegian network. Aker has extensive Norwegian infrastructure experience but AI DC is new territory. Approach could be through Aker's project management function or through Nscale's technical team.
- Scope: Norwegian Execution Readiness Assessment for AI DC
- Duration: 6-8 weeks
- Fee: NOK 2-3M

**Relationship Map:** No existing direct relationship. Aker is well-connected in Norwegian industry — approach through Norconsult leadership or industry networks. Aker may prefer to keep PM in-house given their own capability.

**Competitive Situation:** HIGH competition. Aker has its own project management capability. Nscale may bring international AI DC consultants. However, neither Aker nor Nscale has built a data center in Northern Norway before — the local knowledge gap is real.

**Opportunity Sizing (3 years):**
- Low: NOK 2M (one-off advisory)
- Base: NOK 10-15M (Norwegian execution advisory + grid support for Korgen)
- High: NOK 25M+ (embedded PM for both Narvik and Korgen programs)
