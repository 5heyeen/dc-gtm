## 1. METIER'S INVESTOR VALUE PROPOSITION

### For PE / Infrastructure Funds (e.g., 3i at Lefdal, BGO at Bulk, Apollo at Stack)

**Investor language:** "We de-risk your Norwegian DC CapEx by providing independent execution assurance — quantifying the gap between your development plan and Norwegian market reality before you commit capital, then monitoring delivery against your financial model throughout construction."

**Why they care:**
- PE funds deploying into Norwegian DC for the first time face execution risk they can't assess internally
- A 6-month delay on a 100 MW facility erodes IRR by 400-800 bps — that's the difference between top-quartile and bottom-quartile fund performance
- Norwegian-specific risks (grid timelines, NS contracts, MEP market constraints) are invisible to international investment teams

### For Lenders (Construction Finance)

**Investor language:** "As your Lender's Technical Advisor in Norway, we provide independent construction monitoring backed by the country's largest engineering firm — giving your credit committee the site-specific risk intelligence they need for drawdown certification."

**Why they care:**
- Lenders need independent certification that milestones are achieved before releasing capital
- Norwegian DC construction involves unique risks (grid infrastructure, Norwegian codes, contractor market) that international LTAs may not understand
- Norconsult's engineering depth means the LTA opinion covers both PM AND technical engineering — one firm, one opinion

### For Hyperscalers Allocating Norwegian CapEx

**Investor language:** "We protect your Norwegian investment by ensuring international designs work with Norwegian contractors, Norwegian codes, and Norwegian grid timelines — eliminating the localization surprises that turn a 24-month build into a 36-month build."

### For Family Offices / Sovereign Investors

**Investor language:** "We give you the independent project governance framework that transforms a Norwegian DC development from a speculative bet into a institutional-quality infrastructure asset."

## 2. INVESTOR-GRADE SERVICE PACKAGES

### Package A: Pre-FID Execution Readiness Assessment

| | |
|---|---|
| **Scope** | Independent review of project execution plan, cost estimate, schedule, contractor strategy, and Norwegian market risks |
| **Deliverable** | Investor-grade report: (1) execution risk assessment with quantified IRR impact scenarios, (2) cost estimate validation against Norwegian benchmarks, (3) schedule risk model (P50/P80/P90), (4) contractor market capacity assessment, (5) grid connection timeline validation, (6) regulatory/permitting risk map |
| **Duration** | 4-6 weeks |
| **Team** | 2 Metier seniors (PM, cost) + 2 Norconsult engineers (electrical, grid) + 1 Norconsult environmental |
| **Target Buyer** | PE fund investment team pre-FID; operator seeking investor-grade deliverable; lender during credit review |
| **Fee** | NOK 1.5-2.5M (premium pricing justified by investor-grade standard and independence) |
| **How it leads to more** | Assessment identifies execution gaps → Metier proposes to fill them → construction PM engagement follows |
| **Builds on** | Prompt 1D's pre-FID service design, adapted for investor audience |

### Package B: Lender's Technical Advisor (LTA)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Scope** | Independent technical advisory to lender during construction — drawdown certification, monthly monitoring, risk reporting |
| **Qualification** | LTA roles typically require: (1) engineering capability to assess technical aspects, (2) cost management capability, (3) project monitoring methodology. Metier + Norconsult combined meets all three. No formal certification required — it's reputation and methodology driven. |
| **Duration** | Full construction period (18-36 months) |
| **Team** | 1 Metier senior (PM/cost lead) + 1 Norconsult engineer (monthly site inspections + technical review) |
| **Commercial Model** | Monthly retainer (NOK 150-250K/month) tied to construction duration |
| **Norconsult Engineering Value-Add** | Unique vs. PM-only LTA firms: Norconsult can assess engineering quality (structural, electrical, HVAC) not just schedule/cost. One firm provides both commercial and technical LTA opinion. |
| **Total Engagement Value** | NOK 3-8M per project |

### Package C: Construction Monitoring for Investors

| | |
|---|---|
| **Scope** | Independent monthly reporting to investor/lender: progress vs. plan, cost tracking, risk identification, milestone certification for capital release |
| **How it differs from operator's PM** | We report TO the capital provider, not TO the operator. Our incentive is to identify risk, not to show progress. Structurally independent. |
| **Deliverable** | Monthly investor report (standardized template): schedule status, cost status, risk register updates, photographic evidence, milestone certification |
| **Commercial Model** | Monthly fee (NOK 100-200K/month) + milestone certification fees |
| **Total Engagement Value** | NOK 2-5M per project |

### Package D: Exit Readiness Assessment

| | |
|---|---|
| **Scope** | When investor prepares to sell the DC asset: independent assessment of construction quality, documentation completeness, operational readiness, and defect status |
| **Deliverable** | Technical due diligence report that addresses what a buyer's technical advisor will ask |
| **Duration** | 3-4 weeks |
| **Fee** | NOK 800K-1.5M |
| **Why this matters** | A well-documented, professionally built facility commands a premium at exit. Poor documentation or unresolved defects can reduce value by 5-15% or delay exit. |
| **How it leads to more** | The buyer hires us too — our report IS the starting DD document for the acquirer |
