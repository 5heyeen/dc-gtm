- New hyperscaler entry into Norway (Microsoft greenfield, AWS region) would add NOK 5-10B CAPEX

**Downside risks:**
- Grid bottleneck delays projects (reduces near-term CAPEX deployment)
- Political headwinds slow DC permitting
- Operators build internal Norwegian capability faster than expected
