# Fossefall — Tailored Metier + Norconsult Value Proposition

---

## 1. THE HOOK

**"You have 11 sites, a small team, and 12 months to financial close. How will you demonstrate to investors that you can actually build all of this?"**

Fossefall's critical gap is the jump from startup with a pipeline to credible multi-site developer. They need to show institutional investors (who will be needed to fund 400+ MW) that they have a professional execution framework — not just a spreadsheet of sites. Metier + Norconsult can provide the execution credibility that turns a pitch deck into a fundable program.

**Why this works for Fossefall specifically:**
- CEO Vesterdal comes from investment/energy background — he understands that investors need execution assurance
- Seekr partnership provides demand, but demand without demonstrated delivery capability doesn't attract infrastructure capital
- Every other funded Norwegian DC developer (Bulk, Green Mountain, Nscale/Aker) has either in-house PM capability or established advisory relationships. Fossefall has neither.

## 2. THE VALUE PROPOSITION

The 4 most relevant services for Fossefall right now:

### A. Pre-FID Execution Readiness Assessment (per site)
- Validate each site's feasibility: grid capacity confirmation, building code compliance for AI density, cooling feasibility, fire safety assessment, cost estimate, schedule estimate
- Deliverable: investor-grade site feasibility report per site
- Why Fossefall needs this: they're acquiring brownfield sites fast — each needs independent technical validation before committing capital

### B. Program Delivery Framework Design
- Design a repeatable delivery framework for multi-site brownfield-to-AI-DC conversions
- Standardized project controls, risk registers, contractor procurement templates, reporting structure
- Why Fossefall needs this: 11 sites with SPV structure = governance complexity. A startup cannot scale to 11 parallel projects without a framework. This is exactly what Metier built for Bulk's campus expansion.

### C. Brownfield Conversion Engineering (Norconsult)
- Electrical engineering for AI-grade power density (50-100+ kW/rack)
- Cooling design for AI workloads in converted industrial buildings
- Norwegian building code compliance (TEK17, NEK 400) for facilities not originally designed as DCs
- Fire engineering for high-density compute in converted structures
- Why Fossefall needs this: converting a former childcare center or weapons factory to an AI data center is a specialized engineering challenge. Norconsult has done DC electrical, cooling, and building physics for Bulk, Green Mountain, Lefdal, and Stack.

### D. Investor-Grade Governance & Reporting
- Professional project governance structure suitable for institutional capital raising
- Monthly investor reporting framework, risk dashboards, milestone tracking
- Independent cost and schedule monitoring
- Why Fossefall needs this: when they go to PE/infrastructure funds for growth capital, having Metier-designed governance is a credibility signal. Same model proven with Bulk/BGO relationship.
