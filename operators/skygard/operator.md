## Known Context

> Skygard is a joint venture between Hafslund (energy), Telenor (telecom), and HitecVision (PE/energy). Their strategy is to be the leader in high-security data center solutions in Norway, targeting government, defense, and critical infrastructure clients.
>
> Key implications of their high-security strategy:
> - Contractors and equipment suppliers may need security clearance (sikkerhetsklarering)
> - This constrains their procurement options and makes contract strategy critical
> - Construction processes may need to follow NSM (Nasjonal Sikkerhetsmyndighet) guidelines
> - Comparable international operators in this segment should be researched
>
