Their high-security strategy creates unique procurement and construction challenges (security clearance requirements). This maps to **Pattern B: High-Security Operator** in the pain-point mapping framework.

Primary Metier services to investigate: Contract & Procurement Strategy, Project Execution & PM, Strategy Advisory.
