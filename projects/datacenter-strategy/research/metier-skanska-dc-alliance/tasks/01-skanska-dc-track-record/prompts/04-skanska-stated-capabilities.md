# Prompt 04: skanska-stated-capabilities

> **Prompt:** What does Skanska's corporate website explicitly claim Skanska can do for data center clients? Quote verbatim any dedicated DC service pages, sector pages, or case-study pages. Capture stated service scope (design-build, pre-construction, commissioning, sustainability, modular / prefab, energy integration, heat reuse). Include Skanska's stated sustainability / ESG positioning on DC where stated. Note the page date if visible.

---

**Method note:** Direct `WebFetch` of `usa.skanska.com`, `skanska.co.uk`, `group.skanska.com`, `news.cision.com/skanska`, and most secondary news hosts returned HTTP 403 in this environment. Verbatim quotes below are drawn from search-result snippets that reproduce the on-page text of Skanska's own domains (`usa.skanska.com`, `skanska.co.uk`, `group.skanska.com`, plus Skanska UK's official X/Twitter account) and from Skanska's GlobeNewswire press release. Page-publication dates were not consistently visible in the snippets; noted below only where clearly attributed.

## Inferred Findings

**Inferred:** Skanska's DC go-to-market in the Nordics is positioned primarily around core-and-shell plus site/groundwork execution rather than an integrated design-build-with-sustainability-engineering pitch.
**Based on:**
- "The contract consists of preparatory groundwork and foundations for a new hyperscale data center, including building of new access roads with infrastructure and site development." — [group.skanska.com — Telemark NOK 1.1bn release](https://group.skanska.com/media/press-releases-articles/278070/Skanska-prepares-for-new-data-center-in-Telemark,-Norway,-for-NOK-1.1-billion,-about-SEK-1.1-billion)
- "The contract consists of constructing the core and shell for a new hyperscale data center" — [group.skanska.com — Telemark NOK 569M release](https://group.skanska.com/media/press-releases-articles/284876/Skanska-signs-additional-contract-to-build-a-data-center-in-Telemark,-Norway,-for-NOK-569M,-about-SEK-560M)
**Reasoning:** The only Skanska-owned Nordic DC communications surfaced describe scope as groundwork, core and shell, and site/envelope — with no claims about MEP, commissioning, heat reuse, or sustainability, in contrast with the US (SAT) and UK (M&E fit-out) pages, which do claim those. This pattern suggests the Nordic DC offer is narrower in stated scope than US/UK.

**Inferred:** Skanska's DC sustainability/ESG positioning exists at group level but is not surfaced as DC-specific claims on the dedicated DC pages.
**Based on:**
- "Skanska's strength is delivering right-sized data centers to meet your immediate and future demands." — [Skanska USA — Mission Critical / Data Centers](https://www.usa.skanska.com/what-we-deliver/build/mission-critical--data-centers/)
- No DC-ESG verbatim surfaced on the US DC, UK DC, or Nordic DC contract pages in four searches.
**Reasoning:** Across four targeted queries, zero DC-page text linked DC delivery to Skanska's Color Palette / Net-Zero Carbon / heat-reuse narratives. If such language existed prominently, at least one snippet would likely have surfaced.

- `group.skanska.com/projects/data-centers/` exists as a group DC landing page (surfaced in search index), but its on-page verbatim text could not be retrieved in this pass (403 on fetch).
