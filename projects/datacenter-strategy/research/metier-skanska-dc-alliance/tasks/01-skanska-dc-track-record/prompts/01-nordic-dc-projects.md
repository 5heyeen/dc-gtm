# Prompt 01: nordic-dc-projects

> **Prompt:** Enumerate every publicly reported data center project in Norway, Sweden, Finland, or Denmark where Skanska has been the main contractor, subcontractor, EPCM, or bidder. For each project, capture: (a) client / operator name, (b) location, (c) stated scale (MW or m²), (d) Skanska's contracted role and scope, (e) contract value if disclosed, (f) year awarded and completion year, (g) delivery model. Also check specifically for any Skanska involvement with Bulk / N01 / Fossefall, Green Mountain, Bifrost, Vesten Disse, Digiplex, Stack EMEA, atNorth, or Verne Global (main contractor, subcontractor, or pre-construction). Search Skanska press releases (Skanska.com Norway / Sweden / Finland), Byggeindustrien, Finansavisen, DN, E24, Dagens Industri, Rakennuslehti, Computer Weekly, DCD, and DC Byte.

---

## Summary

Publicly disclosed Nordic Skanska data center work concentrates on **two confirmed projects**: (1) Google's hyperscale campus at Gromstul in Skien (Telemark, Norway), where Skanska (with wholly-owned sub M&D) holds ground/foundations and core-and-shell contracts totalling NOK ~1.7 billion, and (2) a core-and-shell contract for an unnamed "technology company" in Finland booked Q3 2025 at EUR 95M / SEK 1.1bn. No public evidence was found of Skanska serving as contractor to Bulk/N01/Fossefall, Green Mountain, DigiPlex/Stack EMEA, atNorth, Verne Global, Bifrost, or Vesten Disse. No Skanska Denmark data center contract surfaced in public sources.

## Norway

**Google / WS Computing — Gromstul hyperscale campus, Skien, Telemark**

- `"Skanska signed a contract with Google-owned WS Computing AS for preliminary work on their new data center in Skien, and the project is being executed as a joint venture between Skanska Norge and its wholly-owned subsidiary Marthinsen & Duvholt"` — [Byggeindustrien / bygg.no (via search snippet)](https://www.bygg.no/sikret-seg-storkontrakt-for-grunnarbeider-pa-googles-datasenter-i-skien/1545973!/)
- `"The contract is valued at 1.1 billion Norwegian kroner, and covers groundwork and foundations for the new data center at Gromstul in Skien municipality, including site preparation with blasting, filling of masses, concrete work and construction of roads with associated infrastructure."` — [Byggeindustrien / bygg.no (via search snippet)](https://www.bygg.no/sikret-seg-storkontrakt-for-grunnarbeider-pa-googles-datasenter-i-skien/1545973!/)
- `"The overall project represents an investment of approximately 6.8 billion Norwegian kroner (600 million euro) and is planned to become operational in 2026."` — [DLA Piper insight (via search snippet)](https://www.dlapiper.com/en/insights/publications/2024/05/construction-of-googles-eur600-million-hyperscale-data-centre-in-norway)
- `"Groundwork and foundation work began in February 2024."` — [Anleggsmaskinen (via search snippet)](https://anleggsmaskinen.no/2024/02/skanska-skal-utfore-grunnarbeider-for-datasenter/)
- `"Skanska has entered a NOK 569 million contract with WS Computing AS to construct the core and shell of a hyperscale data center in Skien, Norway. The scope of work includes creating the external structure, incorporating wall cladding, windows, doors, roof coverings, and security features such as fencing, with additional ancillary buildings also being developed as part of the project."` — [EuropaWire, Nov 2024](https://news.europawire.eu/skanska-expands-hyperscale-data-center-project-in-norway-with-additional-nok-569m-contract/eu-press-release/2024/11/16/11/54/03/144115/)
- `"Skanska had already signed an earlier contract for this initiative earlier in the year, valued at NOK 1.1 billion, and with the inclusion of this latest agreement, the total contract value has risen to approximately NOK 1.7 billion (around SEK 1.6 billion). Construction activities are set to begin immediately, with the project slated for completion by December 2025."` — [EuropaWire, Nov 2024](https://news.europawire.eu/skanska-expands-hyperscale-data-center-project-in-norway-with-additional-nok-569m-contract/eu-press-release/2024/11/16/11/54/03/144115/)
- `"Bygget er 28 000 m2 fordelt på fire fløyer og en administrasjonsfløy, og arbeidet består av bygging av komplett stålreis, yttervegger og tak. Arbeidet starter umiddelbart og råbygget skal stå ferdig i desember 2025."` — [Byggfakta Nyheter / NTB kommunikasjon (via search snippet)](https://byggfaktanyheter.no/skanska-oppforer-rabygg-til-datasenter-i-skien/)

**Bulk / N01 / Fossefall, Green Mountain, DigiPlex / Stack EMEA**

- No public source found linking Skanska as contractor, sub, EPCM, or disclosed bidder on Bulk N01 (Kristiansand), Fossefall (Fyresdal), Green Mountain (Rjukan / Stavanger / Oslo / Kalberg), or Stack EMEA / DigiPlex (Oslo area). These are catalogued in the Unverified section.

## Inferred Findings

- **Inferred:** The unnamed "technology company" client of the EUR 95M Skanska Finland core-and-shell contract is most likely a hyperscaler (e.g. Microsoft, Google, Meta, AWS) rather than a colo operator.
  **Based on:** `"Skanska signed a contract with a technology company to build a data center facility in Finland for EUR 95M, about SEK 1.1 billion"` — [PRNewswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/skanska-builds-data-center-in-finland-for-eur-95m-about-sek-1-1-billion-302546147.html); `"Microsoft is planning to build a new datacenter region in Southern Finland... locations in both Kirkkonummi, Espoo and Vihti"` — [Microsoft News Centre Europe, March 2022](https://news.microsoft.com/europe/2022/03/17/microsoft-announces-intent-to-build-a-new-datacenter-region-in-finland-accelerating-sustainable-digital-transformation-and-enabling-large-scale-carbon-free-district-heating/).
  **Reasoning:** Skanska's own language ("technology company") plus the fact that publicly-named Finnish colo operators rarely disclose core-and-shell awards at this scale, combined with the active hyperscaler build cycle in Southern Finland (Microsoft, Google Hamina), makes a hyperscaler the most probable counterparty. Identity is not confirmed by the cited sources.

- **Inferred:** The Skien Gromstul project is Skanska's largest publicly disclosed Nordic data center engagement and constitutes the core reference case for a Skanska Nordic DC capability claim.
  **Based on:** `"the total contract value has risen to approximately NOK 1.7 billion (around SEK 1.6 billion)"` — [EuropaWire](https://news.europawire.eu/skanska-expands-hyperscale-data-center-project-in-norway-with-additional-nok-569m-contract/eu-press-release/2024/11/16/11/54/03/144115/); `"Skanska signed a contract with a technology company to build a data center facility in Finland for EUR 95M, about SEK 1.1 billion"` — [PRNewswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/skanska-builds-data-center-in-finland-for-eur-95m-about-sek-1-1-billion-302546147.html).
  **Reasoning:** SEK 1.6bn (Skien cumulative) > SEK 1.1bn (Finland EUR 95M), and no third Nordic Skanska DC contract of comparable scale appears in public sources, so Skien is the largest disclosed Nordic reference.
