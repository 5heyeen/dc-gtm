## Company Structure

### Vergence (Parent — Company Builder)
Vergence is a company builder dedicated to creating lasting value by combining capital, technology, creativity, and commercial expertise. Their thesis: AI is rapidly turning large parts of venture into infrastructure, so what remains when the information advantage is gone is the ability to actually build. Vergence provides Marketer.com with an in-house studio team for creative work. Marketer.com started as a side project inside Vergence in 2022.

### Marketer.com (Primary Product Company)
Marketer.com is the leading AI platform for e-commerce brand owners worldwide who want to scale smarter and faster. The platform turns product catalogs into high-converting, AI-powered ad creatives. It replaces traditional marketing agencies with AI-driven performance marketing.

- **Co-Founder & CEO**: Victor Helgeland
- **HQ**: Oslo, Norway
- **Core markets**: US, Canada, UK, Australia
- **Growth target**: $10M ARR (7-month sprint)

### Manta (AI Marketing Co-Pilot)
Manta is the AI co-pilot within Marketer.com. It turns your data into concrete marketing plans that define scope, actions, and expected impact. Plans are built from your real products and channels, ready to review and run. Available as a standalone web app, on mobile, and directly inside the Marketer platform.

## Products & Services
- **AI-powered ad creatives**: Turns product catalogs into high-converting ad creatives automatically
- **Manta AI co-pilot**: Generates marketing plans from real performance data, products, and channels
- **Ember**: Tool that turns products or brand assets into creatives ready to run
- **Campaign dashboard**: Tracks key metrics for continuous campaign optimization
- **Full-funnel automation**: End-to-end ad management from creative to optimization

## Problems They Solve
- **Agency dependency**: E-commerce brands spend heavily on marketing agencies with inconsistent results. Marketer replaces agencies with AI-driven marketing at a fraction of the cost
- **Creative production bottleneck**: Generating high-converting ad creatives is slow and expensive. The platform automates this from product catalogs
- **Marketing strategy complexity**: Small and mid-sized e-commerce brands lack the data expertise to build effective marketing plans. Manta turns raw data into actionable plans automatically
- **ROAS optimization**: Helps brands achieve significantly higher return on ad spend through AI optimization

## Performance Results (Case Studies)

- Karen Kane: Average 15.7x ROAS with smart automation
