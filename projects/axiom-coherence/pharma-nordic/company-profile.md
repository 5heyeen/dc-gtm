## What They Do
Pharma Nordic is a Nordic pharmaceutical company run by doctors and specialists, focused on innovative and effective solutions in self-care. They hold licenses and distribution rights in the Nordic countries for innovative pharmaceuticals and medical devices that are already marketed elsewhere or are in the process of being launched outside the Nordic region.

## Products & Services
- **Pharmaceutical distribution**: Brings new, well-documented medical products to the Nordic region (Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, Sweden)
- **Medical devices**: Distribution rights for innovative medical devices across the Nordics
- **Key product**: Maxigesic/Dolein — a combination product for pain relief in the Nordic OTC market, positioned as an effective first-line treatment alternative that reduces unnecessary doctor visits

## Problems They Solve
- **Healthcare system pressure**: The Nordic region faces growing pressure from an aging population, increased chronic disease incidence, and shorter hospital stays. Pharma Nordic addresses this by enabling safe and effective self-treatment to relieve the burden on the healthcare system
- **Access to innovation**: Makes innovative medical solutions that are already proven in other markets available to Nordic consumers
- **Self-care gap**: Gives people access to safe, science-based products so they can take better care of their own health without unnecessary medical visits

## Mission
To be the leader in self-care in the Nordics and give people access to safe, science-based products that make life easier and better.
