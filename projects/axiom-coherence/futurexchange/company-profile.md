# FutureXchange (FXC)

## Website
- https://www.fxc.no/

## What They Do
FutureXchange is a Norwegian fintech company that modernises private financial markets. They combine board portals, shareholder administration, and corporate governance tools with efficient capital raising — all in a single digital platform. The company delivers an insight and transaction platform that triggers more efficient capital and competence flow to unlisted companies.

## Products & Services
- **Board portal**: Digital governance tools for company boards
- **Shareholder administration**: Cap table management and shareholder registry
- **DD-room (Due Diligence room)**: Secure data room for investment due diligence
- **Capital raising tools**: Promotional window and investor matching for fundraising
- **Investor Relation Management (IRM)**: Communication and relationship tools for investor engagement
- **Access control**: Full audit trail with top security for sensitive corporate data
- **Investor portfolio view**: Single-login portal giving investors consolidated insight into their portfolio across multiple investments

## Problems They Solve
- **Fragmented private markets**: Unlisted companies struggle with inefficient capital raising, scattered governance tools, and poor shareholder management. FXC consolidates everything into one platform
- **Investor visibility**: Investors in private companies lack easy portfolio oversight — FXC gives them one place to track all holdings
- **Capital-competence matching**: Provides groundbreaking insights and risk-reducing investment models to match capital, competence, and network in the shortest possible time
- **Ownership transparency**: Innovation partnership with Bronnoeysundregistrene (The Norwegian Registry Centre) to use distributed ledger technology for real-time ownership information

## Innovation & Partnerships
- In 2021, entered an innovation contract with Bronnoeysundregistrene for distributed ledger technology integration for real-time ownership tracking
