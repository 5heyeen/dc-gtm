## What They Do
Skipr Technologies is the sovereign interoperability layer for the agentic internet. They enable trusted and secure AI-to-AI communication through encrypted, ephemeral sessions. The company builds an "autonomous trust fabric" enabling secure AI-to-AI interoperability across borders, clouds, and organizations.

## Products & Services

### SecureConnect
A self-deploying, zero-custody connectivity layer for telcos, MVNOs, and governments. Embeds directly into eSIM, roaming, or app networks, replacing VPNs with verified, policy-aware sessions.

### AgentConnect
Creates secure, cryptographically sealed "digital SCIFs" (Sensitive Compartmented Information Facilities) between AI agents and systems. Each interaction verifies identity, policy, and jurisdiction before data moves. Enterprises and governments use it to enable autonomous AI collaboration with full accountability.

## Problems They Solve
- **Insecure AI-to-AI communication**: As AI agents increasingly interact autonomously, there is no trust layer ensuring those interactions are secure, policy-compliant, and auditable. Skipr provides that layer
- **Sovereign data control**: Governments and enterprises need to ensure that data, decisions, and transactions can be shared safely while maintaining sovereign control. Skipr's technology ensures regulatory and national compliance through cryptographic identity and policy-driven routing
- **VPN limitations**: Traditional VPNs are insufficient for modern connectivity needs. SecureConnect replaces them with verified, policy-aware sessions
- **Cross-border AI interoperability**: Enables AI systems to collaborate across borders, clouds, and organizations while maintaining trust and compliance
