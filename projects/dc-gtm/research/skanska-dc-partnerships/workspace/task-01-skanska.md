# Task 01: Skanska DC Partnerships & Alliances Worldwide

**Completed:** 2026-04-21
**Scope:** Named partnerships, alliances, JVs, framework arrangements, and named client relationships in the global DC construction and services market.

---

## Executive Summary

- **Skanska Advanced Technology (SAT)** is a dedicated US-based unit (est. Feb 2025, expanded Sep 2025) with 285+ professionals and 30+ years/240+ DC projects of history; it functions as a de facto relationship management structure for repeat hyperscaler clients, though no alliance names are publicly disclosed.
- **US portfolio signals strong framework relationships**: six+ large contracts (ranging $90M–$263M) awarded to "existing clients" or "repeat clients" in 2024–2026, all with confidential client identities — consistent with master service or preferred-contractor arrangements.
- **Norway (Google/WS Computing, Skien)**: most visible named Nordic engagement — two contracts totaling ~NOK 1.7B for a 28,000m² core & shell, delivered as a joint working arrangement (Skanska Norge + wholly-owned subsidiary Marthinsen & Duvholt).
- **Finland**: EUR 95M contract (Q3 2025) with unnamed "technology company" for core & shell, completion fall 2026 — Nordic expansion continues.
- **UK (SRW)**: named clients VIRTUS (London11 £53M design & build, 2021–23) and Equinix (electrical infrastructure upgrade, Jun 2025, ~1 yr) — repeat-client pattern but no framework publicly named.
- No publicly disclosed formal alliance names, no DC-specific training academies, and no published "governance as a service" products for DC clients found.

## 1. Skanska Advanced Technology (SAT) — Dedicated DC Unit

### Structure
- Launched February 2025 as a dedicated advanced technology group within Skanska USA Building.
- Expanded September 2025 to unite mission-critical/DC capabilities with the semiconductor delivery group.
- 285+ dedicated professionals organized as a "centralized hub for operational oversight, commercial management and resource deployment."
- Operates as a "single point of contact for advanced technology customers nationwide."

### Leadership
- **Anita Nelson** — Executive Officer, SAT and Skanska Integrated Solutions; 20+ years global construction experience; previously Chief Strategy Officer, Skanska USA Building.
- **Katie Coulson** — EVP and General Manager, day-to-day operations of SAT's 285+ team.

### Track Record
- 30+ years of DC construction history.
- 240+ DC and advanced technology projects completed.

### Services (inferred from positioning)
- Design-build and construction management for hyperscale and mission-critical facilities.
- Data hall fit-out, site utilities, MEP coordination.
- Program management through SAT's centralized hub model.
- *No published "governance as a service" or "training as a service" product found.*

## 5. Supply Chain Alliance Programs (General — Not DC-Specific)

Skanska runs several supply chain and subcontractor programs but none are DC-specific:

| Program | Description | Scale |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Construction Management Building Blocks (CMBB) | Since 2007; coaching and education for small/diverse subcontractors working with Skanska | 800+ graduate firms; ~$740M in contracts generated |
