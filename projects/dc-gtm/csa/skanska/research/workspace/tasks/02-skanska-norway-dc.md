# Skanska Norway (Skanska Norge) — Data Center Research Report

*Research conducted April 2026. All claims sourced; inferences labeled accordingly.*

---

## 1. Data Center Projects Completed or Currently Executing in Norway

### Primary Project: Google / WS Computing AS — Gromstul, Skien (Telemark)

This is the dominant and, based on all available public evidence, the **only confirmed major data center construction project** Skanska Norge is executing in Norway as of April 2026.

**Client:** WS Computing AS — a wholly-owned Google subsidiary registered in Norway, acting as the legal counterpart for Google's data center infrastructure investments.

**Location:** Gromstul, north of Skien city centre, Telemark county. Site: approximately 200 hectares, acquired by Google in 2019.

**Total declared Google investment:** €600 million (~NOK 6.8 billion) for a 240 MW hyperscale facility.
Source: [DLA Piper](https://www.dlapiper.com/en-us/insights/publications/2024/05/construction-of-googles-eur600-million-hyperscale-data-centre-in-norway) | [Datacenter Map](https://www.datacentermap.com/norway/skien/google-skien/)

**Skanska's contracts — two phases:**

**Phase 1 (Civil / Groundworks Contract):**
- Signed: Q1 2024
- Value: NOK 1.1 billion (~€96M)
- Scope: Preparatory groundwork, foundations, new access roads, site development, blasting and filling, concrete work
- Construction start: February 2024
- Expected completion: Q4 2025
- Sources: [Skanska Group press release](https://group.skanska.com/media/press-releases-articles/278070/) | [Anleggsmaskinen](https://anleggsmaskinen.no/2024/02/skanska-skal-utfore-grunnarbeider-for-datasenter/) | [Byggeindustrien](https://www.bygg.no/sikret-seg-storkontrakt-for-grunnarbeider-pa-googles-datasenter-i-skien/1545973!/)

**Phase 2 (Core & Shell / Raw Building Contract):**
- Signed: Q4 2024
- Value: NOK 569 million (~€53M)
- Scope: Steel frame/structure, exterior wall cladding, windows, doors, roof coverings; security fencing; smaller technical buildings, guard buildings; outdoor work
- Building specs: 28,000 m² total floor area, four wings plus an administration wing
- Expected completion: December 2025
- Sources: [Skanska Group press release](https://group.skanska.com/media/press-releases-articles/284876/) | [Tungt.no](https://www.tungt.no/article/view/1130391/skanska_oppforer_rabygget_til_googles_datasenter_i_skien)

**Combined Skanska contract value:** ~NOK 1.7 billion

**What Skanska's scope does NOT include:** MEP fit-out, data hall equipment, cooling, UPS/generators, IT infrastructure — handled separately, likely by Google's global specialized DC vendors. [INFERENCE]

## 9. Skanska Norge vs. Local Norwegian Contractors

**Skanska advantages:**
1. First-mover in hyperscale DC — only confirmed NOK 1.7B hyperscale contract in Norway
2. Global DC expertise via Skanska USA (285+ specialists) and Skanska Finland
3. Existing Google relationship — natural candidate for Phase 2 at Gromstul
4. Vertically integrated steel capability (Marthinsen & Duvholt)

**Skanska limitations:**
1. Scope limited to civil + core & shell — no MEP/technical fit-out in Norway (unlike UK via SRW)
2. No published dedicated DC team in Norway (vs. Skanska Finland's explicit DC marketing)
3. Backe has smaller-scale DC experience (Fet datasenter); local contractors may be cost-competitive on smaller projects
4. Pan-Nordic brand — less local relationship advantage vs. Veidekke or AF Gruppen

| Contractor | DC Track Record in Norway |
|---|---|
| **Skanska Norge** | Google Gromstul (NOK 1.7B) — confirmed |
| **Veidekke** | No publicly confirmed hyperscale DC project |
| **AF Gruppen** | No publicly confirmed hyperscale DC project |
| **NCC Norge** | No publicly confirmed hyperscale DC project |
| **Backe** | Fet datasenter (smaller scale) — confirmed |

Source: [Byggvarlden market overview](https://www.byggvarlden.se/storsta-aktorerna-pa-norska-byggmarknaden/)

## Summary

Skanska Norge is the **dominant confirmed hyperscale DC contractor in Norway**, based on the Google/Gromstul project (NOK 1.7B total). Their scope is civil + core & shell only — MEP and technical fit-out are outside their Norway scope. Competitive advantage rests on global DC knowledge transfer from USA/Finland, Marthinsen & Duvholt steel subsidiary, and the Google client relationship. No other major Norwegian DC projects involving Skanska have been publicly confirmed.
