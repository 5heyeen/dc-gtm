## 3. Internal Organization for DC Growth

### Skanska Advanced Technology (SAT)

- **Pre-2025:** Mission-critical/DC team within general US building operations
- **February 2025:** SAT formally launched as distinct unit
- **September 15, 2025:** Major strategic realignment — merged DC construction team with semiconductor/high-tech manufacturing group

**Structure:**
- 285+ dedicated professionals
- Centralized hub for operational oversight, commercial management, resource deployment
- Mobile expert groups deployed across and beyond traditional geographic footprint
- Single point of contact for DC + semiconductor clients

Sources: [USA Skanska](https://www.usa.skanska.com/who-we-are/media/press-releases/301891/), [GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2025/09/15/3149901/0/en/Skanska-Advanced-Technology-Expands-to-Address-Data-Center-and-Semiconductor-Markets.html), [Construction Dive](https://www.constructiondive.com/news/skanska-advanced-tech-growth-unit-data-center/760221/)

Source: [Construction Dive](https://www.constructiondive.com/news/skanska-new-strategy-leadership-ai/802687/)

## 4. Specific Capabilities Being Invested In

### Modular and Prefabricated Construction
- Skid-mounted MEP systems, precast concrete shells, plug-and-play electrical rooms
- Rationale: Faster deployment, scalability, repeatability for multi-campus hyperscale rollouts
Sources: [Construction Dive](https://www.constructiondive.com/news/skanska-advanced-tech-growth-unit-data-center/760221/), [Fall 2025 Market Trends Report](https://www.usa.skanska.com/who-we-are/media/press-releases/303198/)

### Deep Technical / Mission-Critical Expertise
- 30+ years experience, 240+ completed projects with leading global tech/semiconductor companies
- BIM integration, IoT, electrification, energy-efficient cooling, targeting <1.3 PUE in Nordic projects
Source: [GlobeNewswire SAT](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2025/09/15/3149901/0/en/Skanska-Advanced-Technology-Expands-to-Address-Data-Center-and-Semiconductor-Markets.html)

Roving DC and semiconductor subject matter experts deploy to projects beyond Skanska's traditional footprint — geographic scaling without permanent local overhead.

**Skanska's stated differentiators:**
1. Selective, quality-over-volume approach ("competitive advantage, track record, client relationships")

3. 30+ years, 240+ projects track record
4. Sustainability/green leadership — especially in Nordic markets
5. National reach via mobile expert model
6. Semiconductor adjacency — can pursue hybrid DC + chip fab campuses
7. Own AI tools ("Sidekick") demonstrating tech fluency to hyperscale clients

## Summary

**Skanska's DC strategy in one sentence:** Leverage a 30-year track record, deep hyperscale repeat-client relationships, and a unified advanced technology division (SAT) to selectively capture high-margin DC and semiconductor construction contracts across the US and Nordic markets, with modular/prefab delivery as an accelerating enabler.

**Doing explicitly:** SAT created and scaled (Feb 2025, expanded Sep 2025); ~$1B/quarter DC order intake; Nordic expansion (Norway + Finland); modular/prefab investment; mobile expert deployment model.

**Not yet done:** No acquisitions for DC capability; no explicit numeric DC revenue target; no announced UK/CEE DC expansion.
