# DLE Consulting AS — Nordic Data Center Advisory

## Company Overview

**DLE Consulting AS** (also operating as DLE Group) is a Nordic data center advisory firm founded by **Kjetil Ertnaes** (Oslo, Norway) and **Peder Bank** (Copenhagen, Denmark). The firm offers independent senior advisory services across the data center lifecycle, with a combined 40+ years of experience in the data center and colocation business. DLE has a formal partnership with **Grezya**, the European Data Center Consulting Group (see separate profile).

- **Founded:** ~2018 (estimated)
- **HQ:** Oslo, Norway (Kjetil Ertnaes) and Copenhagen, Denmark (Peder Bank)
- **Size:** 2 principals (boutique advisory model, not a large consultancy)
- **Website:** [dle.no](https://www.dle.no/) / [dlegroup.dk](https://www.dlegroup.dk/)
- **Geographic focus:** Nordics (Norway, Denmark, Sweden)

## Market Positioning

DLE operates as a **boutique, independent advisory** — not a project management or engineering firm. They provide strategic and commercial advice to DC operators, investors, and developers in the Nordic market. Their value proposition is deep DC-specific domain knowledge and industry network, not project execution capability.

**Key differentiators:**
- Independent (not tied to any DC operator, equipment vendor, or contractor)
- Deep Nordic DC market knowledge (DigiPlex + Interxion alumni)
- Strong industry network across Nordic and European DC community
- Lean model — senior advisory only, no junior teams or project execution

**Partnership with Grezya** extends their reach across Europe (Italy, Germany, Spain, Poland, UK) while they cover the Nordics.

## Competitive Positioning vs. Metier

| Dimension | DLE Consulting | Metier |
|-----------|---------------|--------|
| **Core competence** | DC strategy, commercial advisory, quality audit | Project management, execution, governance |
| **Service type** | Strategic advisory (pre-decision) | Execution support (during delivery) |
| **Team model** | 2 senior advisors | Full PM teams (PMs, cost controllers, planners) |
| **Scale** | Boutique | 900+ employees |
| **DC specificity** | Deep DC domain (built DigiPlex, Interxion) | Cross-industry PM with growing DC practice |
| **Overlap with Metier** | LOW — complementary, not competitive | N/A |

**Assessment:** DLE is **complementary to Metier, not competitive**. DLE advises on *what* to build and *where* (strategy, market positioning, quality standards). Metier advises on *how* to build it (project management, procurement, execution). In a Kitebrook engagement, DLE/Ertnaes might already be advising on DC strategy, while Metier would fill the execution/PM gap.

**Potential collaboration:** DLE could be a referral source — they advise operators who need PM support but DLE doesn't provide it. Conversely, Metier clients may need DC-specific strategic advice that DLE provides.
