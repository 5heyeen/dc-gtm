# Grezya — The European Data Center Consulting Group

## Company Overview

**Grezya** is a European data center consulting group founded in 2023 by **Luca Venturelli** in Treviolo, Italy. The firm positions itself as a pan-European DC advisory network, operating across key European hubs — Berlin, Milan, Madrid, Poland, and the Nordics — with coverage of emerging markets where digitalization is accelerating. Grezya partners with regional specialists (including **DLE Consulting** in the Nordics) to deliver advisory services across the full DC investment and operational lifecycle.

- **Founded:** 2023
- **HQ:** Treviolo, Italy
- **Size:** Network model — core team + regional partners
- **Website:** [grezya.com](https://www.grezya.com/)
- **Geographic focus:** Pan-European (Italy, Germany, Spain, Poland, Nordics, emerging markets)

## Market Positioning

Grezya operates as a **pan-European advisory network** — not a large consultancy with hundreds of employees, but a curated network of senior DC industry veterans across European markets. Each regional partner brings deep local market knowledge while Grezya provides the pan-European framework.

**Key differentiators:**
- Pan-European coverage through regional partner model (DLE for Nordics, others for Southern/Central Europe)
- Deep DC industry heritage (founders from Digital Realty, Interxion, DigiPlex, Microsoft, Fujitsu)
- M&A due diligence specialization — specifically for DC transactions
- Network of senior advisors rather than scaled consulting teams
- Strong sustainability narrative (Norwegian DC industry association, renewable energy)

**Clients:** Primarily DC investors, operators, and developers evaluating acquisitions, market entry, or strategic positioning across European markets.

## Competitive Positioning vs. Metier

| Dimension | Grezya | Metier |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **Core competence** | DC M&A due diligence, strategy, market intelligence | Project management, execution, governance |
| **Service type** | Investment advisory, strategic advisory | Execution support, PM, owner's engineering |
| **Team model** | Network of senior advisors across Europe | Full PM teams (PMs, cost controllers, planners) |
| **Scale** | Network / boutique | 900+ employees |
| **DC specificity** | 100% DC-focused (pure-play DC advisory) | Cross-industry PM with growing DC practice |
| **Geographic scope** | Pan-European (Italy, Germany, Spain, Poland, Nordics) | Nordics (Norway, Sweden primarily) |
| **Overlap with Metier** | LOW — complementary, not competitive | N/A |

**Assessment:** Grezya is **complementary to Metier, not competitive**. Grezya advises on the investment/strategy side (should we buy this asset? should we enter this market? what's the asset worth?). Metier advises on the delivery side (how do we build it on time and on budget?). There is virtually no service overlap.

**Potential collaboration:** Grezya's M&A clients who acquire DC assets or greenfield sites will need PM support for development and construction — a natural referral pathway to Metier. Grezya's Nordic presence (via DLE/Ertnaes and Bjørn Rønning) makes them a potential channel partner.
