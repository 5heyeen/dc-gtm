# Research Scope

**Scope:** What would it take for Norconsult to work directly for DC operators (rather than as sub-supplier to contractors), and what would it take for Norconsult to localize international data center designs for the Norwegian market?

**Project:** DC GTM (dc-gtm)
**Date:** 2026-04-16

## Specific Questions

1. What would it take for Norconsult to work directly for operators? What are the barriers, capabilities needed, commercial models, and precedents?
2. What would it take for Norconsult to localize international designs (e.g., Schneider reference designs, CTS templates) for Norwegian conditions? What does "localization" actually involve?

## Context from Prior Research

- Norconsult already holds EPCM contract directly with Bulk Infrastructure for N01 campus (600 MW, NOK 3B+)
- COWI works directly for Skygard as "total advisor"
- Traditional Norwegian model: Norconsult as sub-supplier to totalentreprenører (NS 8407)
- DC projects shifting toward owner-led models (EPCM, multi-prime)
- Schneider/Vertiv/Huawei provide reference designs needing site adaptation (civil, grid, TEK17, permitting, fire safety)
- Metier's interest: understanding where they complement vs. overlap with Norconsult
