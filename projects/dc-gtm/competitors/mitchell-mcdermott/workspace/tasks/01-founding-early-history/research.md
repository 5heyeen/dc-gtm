## Section: Application

### When was MMCD founded, by whom, and what was its original service focus?

Mitchell McDermott Construction Consultants Limited was incorporated on **12 January 2015** (Irish CRO company number 555699). It was founded by **Paul Mitchell** and **Anthony McDermott** with equal 50/50 ownership (10 ordinary A shares each).

The company launched as a **full-service, multi-sector construction consultancy** — not a data-center-only firm. Original services: Project Management, Cost Management/Quantity Surveying, Technical Due Diligence, Funder Advisory, Insolvency Services. Early sectors: residential, commercial, data centers, retail, hospitality, student accommodation.

The 10-year anniversary was celebrated in Q1 2025, confirming the 2015 founding.

### Who are the founders and what were their professional backgrounds before MMCD?

**Paul Mitchell** spent ~25 years at **Davis Langdon** (globally the world's largest QS firm before AECOM's $324M acquisition in 2010). Two years in San Francisco; became Head of Ireland for Cost and Project Management and sat on the UK/Ireland Board post-acquisition. Holds a postgrad in construction law from Trinity College Dublin.

**Anthony McDermott** has 26+ years of construction industry experience. Career path: leading Irish practice → 6 years at **Page Kirkland (Sydney)** → **Davis Langdon London** → two international cost consultancies in **Abu Dhabi** → returned to Davis Langdon/AECOM Ireland in 2007, becoming Director and Head of Cost Management for Ireland in 2010.

**Key insight:** Both founders were senior co-directors at Davis Langdon/AECOM Ireland simultaneously (2010–2015) before jointly departing to found MMCD. This was a classic high-level professional services spin-out from a global firm's Irish practice.

Neither founder came from the contracting side — both are cost management / project management consultants. This positions MMCD as an **owners' advisor / PMC**, not a general contractor or M&E firm.

### Was MMCD always focused on data centers, or did it start with a broader construction/engineering scope?

MMCD was **not exclusively DC-focused at founding**. Early staff (2015 hires) worked across residential, hospitality, retail, commercial, and student accommodation alongside data centers. 

The firm's explicit DC thought leadership (quarterly Data Centre Infocards) began in **2019**, with the earliest PDF archived being January 2019. This suggests the DC specialization was progressive:

| Period | DC Focus |
|--------|----------|
| 2015 founding | Multi-sector, DC among equals |
| 2016–2018 | DC growing prominence |
| 2018–2019 | Active DC thought leadership begins |
| 2020+ | Explicit DC consultancy positioning |
| 2025 | DC is co-flagship sector alongside residential |

*Inference:* Ireland's hyperscale DC boom (2016–2020, driven by Amazon, Google, Meta, Microsoft) created the pull toward DC specialization. MMCD was well-positioned to ride this wave having built early DC relationships.
