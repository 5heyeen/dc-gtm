## Founding Date and Incorporators

Mitchell McDermott Construction Consultants Limited was incorporated on **12 January 2015** under Irish company registration number **555699**. The company was founded by **Paul Mitchell** and **Anthony McDermott**, who each hold 10 ordinary A shares (50/50 ownership split).

- Source: [SoloCheck — Mitchell McDermott Construction Consultants Limited](https://www.solocheck.ie/Irish-Company/Mitchell-Mcdermott-Construction-Consultants-Limited-555699)
- Source: [Vision-Net — Mitchell McDermott Construction Consultants Limited](https://www.vision-net.ie/Company-Info/Mitchell-Mcdermott-Construction-Consultants-Limited-555699)

The company's 10-year anniversary was celebrated in Q1 2025, confirmed by their Q2 2025 newsletter which stated they "marked 10 years in business this year."

- Source: [Q2 2025 Newsletter — Mitchell McDermott](https://mitchellmcdermott.com/q2-2025-newsletter/)

## Headquarters

Dublin, Ireland. As of 2025, they moved to new headquarters at 2 Shelbourne Buildings, Ballsbridge, Dublin — doubling their Dublin office footprint.

## Original Service Offering

Mitchell McDermott was established as a **full-service construction consultancy**, not solely a data center firm. Their founding service offering spanned:

- **Project Management**
- **Cost Management / Quantity Surveying**
- **Technical Due Diligence**
- **Funder Advisory Services**
- **Insolvency Services**
- **Construction Consultancy**

They described themselves as serving "all sectors of the construction industry," with early work spanning residential, commercial, data centers, retail, hospitality, and student accommodation.

- Source: [Mitchell McDermott — About](https://mitchellmcdermott.com/about/)
- Source: [Mitchell McDermott — DCD profile](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/company/mitchell-mcdermott/)

## Sector Focus from Founding

*Inference (labeled):* Data centers were part of the offering from year one (staff hired in 2015 worked across multiple sectors including data centers), but the firm was positioned as a multi-sector construction consultancy. The explicit specialization in data centers appears to have intensified as the Irish DC market boomed post-2016, rather than being an exclusive focus at founding.

- Source: [Mitchell McDermott — Careers page](https://mitchellmcdermott.com/careers/) (staff bios confirm 2015 hires worked across residential and data centers)
