# Prompt 02: founders-professional-backgrounds-before-mmcd

> **Prompt:** Who are the founders and what were their professional backgrounds before MMCD? Look for names on the MMCD website, LinkedIn, and any press interviews. What firms did they work at before founding MMCD? Did they come from general contractors (e.g., Mercury Engineering, Jones Engineering), specialist DC firms, or the client side?

---

## Paul Mitchell — Co-Founder & Director

Paul Mitchell spent approximately **25 years at Davis Langdon**, a leading global quantity surveying and construction consultancy. His trajectory there:

- Spent approximately 2 years in **San Francisco** with Davis Langdon
- After AECOM acquired Davis Langdon in 2010, Paul became **Head of Ireland for Cost and Project Management** and sat on the **UK/Ireland Board**
- As of October 2012 he was titled **Director and Head of Office for Ireland** at Davis Langdon, An AECOM Company
- Holds a postgraduate qualification in **construction law** from **Trinity College Dublin**
- He left AECOM to co-found Mitchell McDermott in early 2015

Paul's background is firmly in **cost management and project management consultancy** (not contracting). Davis Langdon was the world's largest quantity surveying firm before its acquisition by AECOM in 2010 for US$324 million.

## Anthony McDermott — Co-Founder & Director

Anthony McDermott brings over **26 years of construction industry experience**, with an international career:

1. Early career at a **leading Irish construction practice** (unnamed in public sources)
2. Moved to **Sydney, Australia** — spent **6 years** at **Page Kirkland** (a quantity surveying consultancy)
3. Moved to **London** with **Davis Langdon**
4. Moved to **Abu Dhabi** — worked at **two international cost consultancies**
5. **Returned to Davis Langdon (Ireland) in 2007** — rose to become **Director and Head of Cost Management for Ireland** in 2010

Both founders were therefore **senior directors at Davis Langdon / AECOM Ireland simultaneously in 2010–2015** — making their co-founding of Mitchell McDermott effectively a joint departure from the same global firm to create a leaner, independent Irish alternative.

## Classification: Consultancy, Not Contracting

**Neither founder came from the contracting side of construction.** Their backgrounds are in:
- Quantity Surveying (cost management)
- Project Management consultancy
- Funder advisory / Technical Due Diligence

This makes MMCD a **construction consultancy** (owners' advisor / PMC), not a general contractor or mechanical/electrical contractor. *Inference:* The "Mitchell McDermott" brand benefits from the same name pattern as large construction firms (e.g., Turner & Townsend, Gardiner & Theobald) but operates in a different segment than EPC contractors like Mercury Engineering or Jones Engineering.
