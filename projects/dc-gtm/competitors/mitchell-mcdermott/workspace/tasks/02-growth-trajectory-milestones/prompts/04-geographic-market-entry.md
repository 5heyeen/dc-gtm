> **Prompt:** What geographic markets did MMCD enter and when? (Ireland, UK, Netherlands, Germany, Spain, Malaysia)

## Geographic Expansion Timeline

### Ireland — Dublin (2015, founding)
MMCD's home market. Ireland is its dominant revenue base. Ireland became Europe's leading hyperscale data center market between 2016–2022, driven by tax incentives (12.5% corporate tax), English language, and EU base for hyperscalers. MMCD was perfectly positioned in Dublin to ride this boom.

### Ireland — Limerick (September 2024)
Regional expansion driven by residential and commercial project demand in Munster/Mid-West. Primarily non-DC work.

### UK (date uncertain — likely ~2019–2021)
MMCD lists "hubs" in the UK. UK is the second-largest European DC market (London Docklands cluster). Consistent with MMCD expanding DC advisory work into UK hyperscale projects. No specific office opening announcement found.

*Inference:* UK presence likely started informally with staff working on cross-border projects before a formal hub was established. The UK was a natural second market given MMCD founders' prior Davis Langdon connections in London.

### Netherlands — Amsterdam (Early 2025)
Europe's third-largest DC market and a major hyperscale hub. First formal continental European office. Explicitly stated as a Benelux + mainland Europe DC expansion beachhead.

### Germany (date uncertain)
MMCD lists a Germany "hub." Germany is Europe's largest DC market by some measures (Frankfurt is a Tier 1 DC hub). No specific opening announcement found publicly.

*Inference:* Germany presence likely follows Amsterdam office as a natural next step given Frankfurt's DC market size. May be a single representative/project-based presence rather than a full staffed office.

### Spain (date uncertain)
MMCD lists a Spain "hub." Spain (Madrid, Barcelona) is a growing European DC market receiving significant hyperscale investment. No specific opening details found.

### Malaysia (date uncertain)
MMCD lists a Malaysia "hub" (Kuala Lumpur). Southeast Asia is a rapidly growing DC market. Several hyperscalers have made major investments in Malaysia (Microsoft $2.2B commitment 2024, Google, Amazon). 

*Inference:* The Malaysia presence likely reflects MMCD following Irish or European clients (possibly Meta or a hyperscale operator) into the APAC market. Ireland-based DC consultancies have followed their hyperscale clients to new regions as those clients expand globally.
