## Revenue Growth Rate

*Inference:* If €300K in 2015 → €5.3M in 2025, that's approximately **17× growth over 10 years**, or a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of ~33%. This is very strong for a professional services firm — driven primarily by Ireland's and Europe's data center construction boom.
