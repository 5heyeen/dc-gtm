## Strategic Significance of Awards

The award pattern tells a clear story:

1. **PM Firm of the Year × 3 (2020–2022)** → establishes MMCD as Ireland's leading PM firm in the mid-market, legitimises its position with institutional clients (banks, developers, hyperscalers)

2. **Workplace culture awards (2021–2024)** → talent attraction in a tight construction labour market. Paul Mitchell explicitly stated that culture is "the top accolade" — because highly skilled construction PMs "could get a job elsewhere in the morning"

3. **Consultancy of the Year 2025** → signals MMCD has evolved beyond PM-only to a full-service advisory brand, consistent with its pitch to PE investors and hyperscale clients

4. **DEI awards** → in a heavily male-dominated industry, 36% female team is a genuine differentiator for clients with ESG/diversity requirements (hyperscalers with public diversity commitments)
