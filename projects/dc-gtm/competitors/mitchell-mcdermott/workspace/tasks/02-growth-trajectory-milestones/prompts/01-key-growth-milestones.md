# Prompt 01: key-growth-milestones-since-founding-2015

> **Prompt:** What were the key growth milestones for MMCD since founding in 2015? (office openings, new service lines, headcount growth, revenue growth, awards won)

---

## Key Growth Milestones: Mitchell McDermott (2015–2025)

### 2015 — Founding
- Incorporated 12 January 2015 by Paul Mitchell and Anthony McDermott
- Dublin office only; ~2–5 staff including founders
- Multi-sector consultancy: PM, QS/Cost Management, Due Diligence, Funder Advisory, Insolvency
- Early hires (Conor Dempsey, Dean Elcock, Adrian Lynch) join in 2015 — all promoted to Divisional Directors by 2025
- Source: [Mitchell McDermott About](https://mitchellmcdermott.com/about/), [Business Post — Appointments](https://www.businesspost.ie/commercial-reports/appointments-at-mitchell-mcdermott/)

### ~2016–2018 — Establishing DC Pipeline
- Data centre work grows alongside residential and commercial pipeline
- Ireland hyperscale DC boom begins (Amazon, Google, Microsoft, Facebook expanding in Dublin)
- *Inference:* MMCD begins winning DC project mandates through founder relationships carried over from Davis Langdon/AECOM
- First quarterly "Data Centres Infocard" published by January 2019 — indicates established DC expertise by 2018
- Source: [Data Centres Infocard Jan 2019 (PDF)](https://mitchellmcdermott.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Mitchell-McDermott-Data-Centres-Infocard-Jan19.pdf)

### 2019 — First Infocard Published / DC Thought Leadership Launch
- January 2019: First "Data Centres Infocard" published — establishes MMCD as a DC market intelligence voice in Ireland
- Positions the firm alongside Digital Infrastructure Ireland (DII) as a sector knowledge source
- Source: [Data Centres Infocard Jan 2019](https://mitchellmcdermott.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/11/Mitchell-McDermott-Data-Centres-Infocard-Jan19.pdf)

### 2020 — First Major Award Win
- Won **Project Management Firm of the Year** at Irish Construction Industry (ICI) Awards — first win
- Source: [Building Ireland Magazine](https://buildingirelandmagazine.com/mitchell-mcdermott-retain-project-management-firm-of-the-year-award/)

### 2021 — Culture Recognition + Award Repeat
- Won **Project Management Firm of the Year** at ICI Awards — second consecutive win
- Joint win: **Tourism & Hospitality Project of the Year** (Zanzibar Locke restoration project with Bennett Construction)
- First certified as **Great Place to Work** Ireland — signals investment in culture and talent retention
- Source: [Building Ireland Magazine](https://buildingirelandmagazine.com/mitchell-mcdermott-retain-project-management-firm-of-the-year-award/)

### 2022 — Three-in-a-Row + European Recognition
- Won **Project Management Firm of the Year** at ICI Awards — third consecutive win (December 2022)
- Ranked **1st in Ireland's Best Workplaces** (small firm category, Great Place to Work Ireland)
- Recognised at **Best Workplaces Europe 2022** (Venice) — 28th in medium-sized businesses
- Source: [Building Ireland Magazine](https://buildingirelandmagazine.com/mitchell-mcdermott-retain-project-management-firm-of-the-year-award/), [Irish Times — Home-grown International Success](https://www.irishtimes.com/special-reports/2023/02/23/home-grown-international-success-stories/)

### 2023 — 65 Staff / International Profile Grows
- **65 employees** reported (February 2023 Irish Times feature)
- Ranked **2nd in Ireland's Best Workplaces 2023** (small firms)
- Ranked **39th in Best Workplaces Europe 2023**
- Annual Construction Sector Report published (2023)
- Source: [Irish Times — Home-grown International Success](https://www.irishtimes.com/special-reports/2023/02/23/home-grown-international-success-stories/)

### 2024 — Limerick Office / 70+ Staff
- **September 19, 2024**: Limerick office opened at Bedford Row — 4 staff at launch, grew to 8 within weeks
- Eoin McEnery heads Limerick office (previously appointed Divisional Director 2025)
- Company employs **70+ construction professionals** as of late 2024
- Ranked **9th in Best Workplaces Ireland 2024**
- Source: [Limerick Post](https://www.limerickpost.ie/2024/10/05/construction-companys-new-limerick-office-laying-building-blocks-for-success/), [ICI Awards 2024](https://www.constructionawards.ie/news/irish-construction-industry-awards-2024-celebrating-excellence-in-construction)

### 2025 — 10th Anniversary + Amsterdam + PE Investment
- Q1 2025: **10th anniversary** — new Dublin HQ at 2 Shelbourne Buildings, Ballsbridge (doubles footprint)
- Q1 2025: Annual Client Event — four new Divisional Directors announced
- Early 2025: **Amsterdam office** opened (Benelux/mainland Europe hub for DC and PM work)
- **~76 employees** across Dublin, Limerick, Amsterdam + hubs in UK, Germany, Spain, Malaysia
- **October 2025**: MML Ireland PE investment announced
- December 2025: Won **Consultancy of the Year** + **Diversity, Equality & Inclusion Strategy of the Year** at ICI Awards
- Total DC capacity served: **400+ megawatts** (completed + ongoing)
- Source: [Q2 2025 Newsletter](https://mitchellmcdermott.com/q2-2025-newsletter/), [Construction Wave](https://constructionwave.co.uk/2025/10/08/mitchell-mcdermott-private-equity-deal-will-unlock-expansion-into-european-data-centre-market/)
