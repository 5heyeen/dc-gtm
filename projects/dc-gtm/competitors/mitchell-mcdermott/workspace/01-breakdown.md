# Breakdown: Mitchell McDermott DC Market Rise

**Date:** 2026-04-16

## Context
Research how Mitchell McDermott (MMCD), an Irish data center contractor/project delivery firm, grew to become a dominant player in the European and global data center construction market. This research supports Metier's GTM strategy — understanding MMCD's playbook helps identify what worked, what didn't, and where gaps remain.

## Subtasks

1. **Founding & Early History** `[independent]` — Research MMCD's founding story, founders' backgrounds, original service offering, and early company trajectory (pre-2015)
2. **Growth Trajectory & Milestones** `[independent]` — Research headcount growth, revenue progression, geographic expansion timeline, and key company milestones
3. **Market Strategy & Positioning** `[independent]` — Research how MMCD positioned in the DC market, early niches targeted, expansion strategy, and go-to-market approach
4. **Key Clients & Partnerships** `[independent]` — Research which hyperscalers, colo providers, and operators MMCD has worked with and how those relationships developed
5. **Challenges & Problem-Solving** `[independent]` — Research obstacles faced (supply chain, talent, scale, regulation) and how they were resolved
6. **Competitive Differentiation** `[independent]` — Research how MMCD differentiates from Turner, Mace, Mercury Engineering, and other DC contractors
7. **Key Success Factors & Synthesis** `[sequential]` — Synthesize from all prior research what the critical success factors were for MMCD's rise (depends on 1–6)
