## Known Context

Kitebrook is J. Byrne Murphy's "DigiPlex 2.0" — a new data center platform launched 2024 after selling DigiPlex (Scandinavia's largest DC operator) in 2021. Self-funded by Murphy and Bill Conway (Carlyle Group co-founder). 130 MW pipeline across 3 sites in western Norway, all pre-construction. Only 1-4 employees in Norway. No EPC, no engineering firm, no PM engaged. PM/Owner's Engineer opportunity is wide open — no competitor visible. Key risk: Murphy's deep DC expertise means he won't be impressed by generic pitches. ASP Data Center is a direct geographic competitor moving faster in Vestland.
