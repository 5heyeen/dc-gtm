# Company Research & Corporate Structure: Kitebrook
## Date: 2026-04-08

### Company Overview

**Kitebrook Partners** is a property development and investment company headquartered in Washington, D.C. (5335 Wisconsin Ave NW), headed by **J. Byrne Murphy**, one of the founding principals of McArthurGlen Europe. In 2024, Kitebrook launched a data center platform in the Nordic region to develop infrastructure for AI-enabled data centers, leveraging abundant hydroelectric power for large-scale, green-powered DC solutions in 100+ MW tranches.

**Key predecessor venture**: In 2001, Murphy and **Bill Conway** (Co-founder and Co-Chairman of The Carlyle Group) acquired Norwegian DC operator **DigiPlex** during the dot-com bust. Over 20 years, they built it into Scandinavia's largest data center operator. DigiPlex was sold to **IPI Partners** in August 2021 and now operates as **STACK Infrastructure**.
