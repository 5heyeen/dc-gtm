# Socratic Research Prompts: Ramboll DC Services Catalog

> **Topic:** Map all data center services that Ramboll (including EYP MCF and i3 Solutions) explicitly markets to data center clients — strategy, design, engineering, sustainability, commissioning, operations.
> **Use these prompts in sequence in any AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) for deep, structured research.**

---

## Principles

- `[independent]` What makes a complete, credible data center service catalog for a large engineering consultancy — what service lines must be present, and which are optional differentiators?
- `[independent]` How do professional services firms typically structure and present their service catalogs — by client journey, by discipline, by project phase, or by outcome?
- `[independent]` What is the difference between services a firm actually delivers vs. services it markets — what signals in a service catalog indicate genuine vs. aspirational capability?
- `[independent]` How do multi-entity firms (parent + acquired subsidiaries) typically reconcile overlapping or duplicate service offerings in their combined catalog?
