The September 2025 Ramboll white paper (from i3/Ansett) covers sustainability, not AI density. This may be a gap.

## Overall Verdict

Based on available public evidence, Ramboll's integration of EYP MCF has NOT failed. The primary uncertainty is whether EYP MCF's technical methodology has kept pace with the AI/liquid cooling transition — this is not verifiable from public sources and would require direct engagement to assess.
