# Prompt 26: eyp-playbook-published-descriptions

> **Prompt:** Search for "EYP data center playbook" OR "EYP mission critical playbook" OR "Ramboll EYP data center methodology" — what published descriptions exist?

---

## Key Finding: No Single Published "Playbook" Document

EYP Mission Critical Facilities does NOT appear to have published a document explicitly titled a "playbook." The methodology is described across their website and third-party sources but not as a downloadable framework document.

## The "Hybrid IT and Adaptive Stack" Framework

From eypmcfinc.com, EYP describes its approach as a **"Hybrid IT and Adaptive Stack" framework**:

> "Being able to advise clients on the right hybrid IT or adaptive stack plan takes proven methodologies and hands-on experience."

The methodology starts with understanding clients' business requirements and challenges, then assists in making strategic decisions across the entire technology, facilities, and operational landscapes to define the best Hybrid IT and Adaptive Stack strategy.

Service scope described as: "from strategic planning and roadmaps through complete engineering design and integration to commissioning, operations, and energy consulting" — a "holistic set of offerings."

Sources: [eypmcfinc.com Home](https://www.eypmcfinc.com/), [eypmcfinc.com Services](https://www.eypmcfinc.com/services), [eypmcfinc.com About](https://www.eypmcfinc.com/about)

## Ramboll.com Description

Ramboll's data center consulting page describes a **risk-based approach to site due diligence**, factoring in power, environmental, zoning, connectivity, access, and sustainability impacts. They describe customizing "every project to meet specific technology and data center objectives with a maintenance-friendly, scalable and energy-efficient design."

Source: [Ramboll Data Center Consulting](https://www.ramboll.com/en-us/technology/data-centers-mission-critical-facilities)

## "Sourcing and Demand Analysis" Methodology

DCD documented EYP MCF adding a "new service delivery model focused on strategic technology planning," describing a **multistep Sourcing and Demand Analysis methodology**: EYP uses "a powerful analysis methodology to create a set of comparative solutions for a multiyear data center sourcing solution, allowing for the collaborative creation of a strategy that uses the best alternatives for managing your application portfolio and investing in the right options over time."

Source: [EYP MCF adds new service delivery model — DCD](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/eyp-mission-critical-facilities-adds-new-service-delivery-model-focused-on-strategic-technology-planning/)

## Assessment

The "EYP playbook" as described in industry discussions is a marketing shorthand for their proprietary delivery approach — not a single published document. The actual methodology elements include:
- Hybrid IT / Adaptive Stack strategic advisory framework
- Sourcing and Demand Analysis multistep methodology
- Probability Risk Analysis (PRA) for site auditing
- CFD-based thermal modeling
- IUE metric contribution (extends PUE)
- Modular/prefabricated construction approach
