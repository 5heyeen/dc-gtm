## White Papers

**IUE (Infrastructure Usage Effectiveness) White Paper — The Green Grid**
Yigit Bulut of EYP MCF contributed to the development of the IUE metric, extending PUE to include power delivery, cooling generation, cooling flow, and rack capacity utilization.
Source: [IUE White Paper — DCD](https://media.datacenterdynamics.com/media/documents/IUE_White_Paper-Final.pdf)

**"Infrastructure Sustainability Options and Revenue Opportunities for Data Centres" — i3 Solutions Group & EYP MCF (2022)**
First in a planned series on GHG abatement for data center operators; covers how GHG reduction targets and revenue generation are not mutually exclusive.
Source: [i3 Solutions Group and EYP MCF white paper](https://datacentre.solutions/news/60967/i3-solutions-group-and-eyp-mission-critical-facilities-launch-data-centre-sustainability-white-paper)
