# Prompt 25: key-eyp-leaders-retention

> **Prompt:** Who were the key EYP leaders in the data center practice, and did they stay with Ramboll post-acquisition?

---

## Key Leadership Confirmed Post-Acquisition

**Rick Einhorn — Managing Partner**
- One of eight former EYP principals who led the 2018 MBO from HPE
- Served as Worldwide VP of Data Center Consulting at HPE during the HP ownership period
- Remained as Managing Partner post-Ramboll acquisition — confirmed by post-acquisition quotes and LinkedIn
- Has authored opinion content on DCD: [DCD profile](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/opinions/?profile=rick-einhorn)
- LinkedIn: [Rick Einhorn — Ramboll](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rick-einhorn-a5a3511b/)

**Brian Whelan, PMP — Managing Partner**
- Part of the 2018 MBO group; held COO title during HPE era; became Managing Partner post-independence
- Focus: commissioning, project management, operations consulting
- [DCD profile](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/profile/brian-whelan/)
- LinkedIn: [Brian Whelan, PMP](https://www.linkedin.com/in/brian-whelan-pmp-199baa10/)

**Yigit Bulut — Partner / Chief Technology Officer / Sr. Technical Manager, Electrical Engineering**
- 35+ years experience in project management and electrical engineering for data centers and mission-critical facilities
- Active conference presence: presented/moderated at 7x24 Exchange Fall 2024
- Contributed to The Green Grid's IUE (Infrastructure Usage Effectiveness) metric white paper
- [7x24 Exchange Fall 2024 profile](https://conferences.7x24exchange.org/fall2024/yigit-bulut/)

**Spiros Liolis — Senior Data Center Consultant & Chief Technologist, Strategy**
- 20+ years experience; drives sustainable growth plans and business development
- Active conference presence: presented at 7x24 Exchange Fall 2023
- [7x24 Exchange Fall 2023 profile](https://conferences.7x24exchange.org/fall2023/spiros-liolis/)

**Jim Davis — Principal, Sustainability & Resilience**
- Focus on sustainable and resilient data center design
- Panelist at DCD>Connect New York (March 2024) on regulation, innovation, and sustainability

## Historical Figure (Departed)

**Peter Gross, PE — Founder (historical; departed pre-Ramboll)**
- Co-founder and original CEO of EYP Mission Critical Facilities (pre-HP entity)
- Left EYP after the HP era; now founder/principal of PMG Associates Consulting and Advisory
- INFERENCE: Not confirmed to be part of Ramboll/EYP MCF post-2022; appears to have departed earlier

## Key Observation

All available evidence indicates the core leadership team (Einhorn, Whelan, Bulut, Liolis, Davis) remained with EYP MCF post-Ramboll acquisition. The firm operates as a distinct brand entity with separate website, which supports leadership continuity — the brand preservation strategy is consistent with key person retention.

In October 2025, EYP MCF (Part of Ramboll) was selected for a major AI campus project in Texas, confirming the practice is commercially active three years post-acquisition.
