# Prompt 23: press-releases-strategic-narrative

> **Prompt:** What press releases, news articles, or announcements from Ramboll or EYP described the acquisition — what strategic narrative did each firm use?

---

## Ramboll's Narrative

Ramboll framed the deal as a sustainability-forward move to capture the intersection of digitalization and climate transition.

COO Michael Simmelsgaard's quote: *"Two huge trends are shaping our world today: the climate crisis and the digitalization of society. As digitalization advances...data centers have a critical role to play in supporting a green circular economy... our role is to ensure that the facilities we design, build and operate on behalf of our clients are at the cutting edge of environmental performance."*

Ramboll emphasized:
- Becoming a top-tier global data center consultancy
- 100% renewably powered, zero-waste data centers as an achievable target
- Combined ~100 dedicated data center specialists globally
- USD 60 million combined revenue target by 2025

## EYP MCF's Narrative

EYP MCF framed the deal as a global scale opportunity for their existing client base.

Managing Partner Rick Einhorn's quote: *"Joining Ramboll is a great opportunity to expand our consulting and engineering services to our existing global client base while bringing our technical expertise into Ramboll's existing and future clients across 35 countries around the world."*

EYP MCF messaging emphasized continuity — leadership team remained in place, operations continued under the EYP MCF brand (sub-branded as "EYP Mission Critical Facilities, Part of Ramboll").

## Post-Acquisition Brand Structure

EYP MCF retained its brand identity as "EYP Mission Critical Facilities, Part of Ramboll" with a separate website (eypmcfinc.com) — a deliberate strategy of brand preservation rather than full absorption, consistent with how Ramboll typically integrates acquisitions.
