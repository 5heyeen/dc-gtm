# Prompt 24: ramboll-eyp-brand-integration

> **Prompt:** After the acquisition, how did Ramboll rebrand or integrate EYP — is EYP still a named brand within Ramboll, or was it absorbed under the Ramboll name?

---

## Result: Dual Brand — EYP Preserved as Sub-Brand

EYP was **NOT fully rebranded under the Ramboll name**. It operates under a dual-brand identity: **"EYP Mission Critical Facilities, Part of Ramboll."**

**Evidence:**
- Maintains independent website at **eypmcfinc.com** (separate from ramboll.com)
- LinkedIn company page (2025): "EYP Mission Critical Facilities, Part of Ramboll"
- Business Wire press releases (October 2025) still refer to "EYP Mission Critical Facilities, Part of Ramboll Group A/S"
- Ramboll's main website hosts a separate data center consulting page (ramboll.com/en-us/technology/data-centers-mission-critical-facilities) referencing the combined practice
- Ramboll thought leadership hub: "Transforming the future of data centres" (ramboll.com/transforming-the-future-of-data-centres)

## Full Ownership Timeline

- **1996:** EYP Mission Critical Facilities Group founded within Einhorn Yaffee Prescott Architecture and Engineering
- **2007/2008:** Acquired by Hewlett-Packard (HP)
- **2018:** MBO by former EYP principals (Rick Einhorn, Brian Whelan, and others); HPE retained overseas practices
- **May 2021:** TA Associates PE investment (~two-thirds stake)
- **January 31, 2022:** Acquired by Ramboll Group A/S
- **September 2024:** Ramboll also acquires **i3 Solutions Group** (UK/Singapore, founded by Ed Ansett), adding European/Asian expertise. Combined practice now has "hundreds of dedicated data centre design experts globally."

## What ramboll.com Shows

- **ramboll.com/en-us/technology/data-centers-mission-critical-facilities** — end-to-end data center consulting, combining EYP MCF's strategy/planning/design/commissioning with Ramboll's broader sustainable building, energy, environment, and human health practices
- **ramboll.com/transforming-the-future-of-data-centres** — thought leadership hub using combined Ramboll+EYP identity
- **ramboll.com/en-us/news/nycw-2025-developing-sustainable-data-centers** — September 2025 net-zero roadmap report

## Assessment

Three years post-acquisition (2025), EYP MCF retains full brand independence as a named sub-entity within Ramboll. This is a deliberate strategy consistent with A/E best practice for retaining key personnel and client relationships. The separate eypmcfinc.com website and distinct brand identity suggest Ramboll has not yet (and may never fully) absorb EYP into the Ramboll corporate identity.
