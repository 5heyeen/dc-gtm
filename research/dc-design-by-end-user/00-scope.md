# Research Scope

**Scope:** What exactly needs to be designed in a data center, and how do those designs change depending on the end user (CoreWeave, AI training, inference, hyperscaler, sovereign, defense, etc.)? What is it about end-user requirements that dictates engineering design? How do standardized designs (e.g. CTS) fail to meet specific client needs, causing delays and redesign?

**Project:** DC GTM (Data Center Go-to-Market)
**Date:** 2026-04-03
