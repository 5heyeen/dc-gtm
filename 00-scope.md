**Scope:** Data Center Go-To-Market Plan for Metier AS — defining what services to sell, to whom, when, why, and how in the Nordic data center market. Grounded on competitive analysis, customer persona mapping, pain point identification, market evolution research, and Metier + Norconsult combined capability assessment.

**Project:** Data Center Market Analysis (Oppdrag 2025.11.27 Bulk Datacenters / MTR_OR_Datasenter Markedsanalyse_1593)
